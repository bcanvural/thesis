Position Description

The purpose of this job is to identify and deliver results on high - impact
quality and rework reduction opportunities leveraging big data analytics and
machine learning. In this position, the individual will be advocating the power
of data science concepts and working with cross - functional teams to create
actionable insight across core business lines. They will utilize their advanced
data analytic skills to identify relevant process and knowledge needed to solve
large cross - functional quality and rework problems and will propose solutions
to close these gaps.

 

Primary Responsibilities:

  * Discover the cross - functional enterprise wide, end - to - end information
    hidden in vast amounts of data, and help the Enterprise make smarter
    decisions to reduce rework and improve issue resolution
  * Deliver a Big Data Analytics solution utilizing tools like MapR Hadoop,
    Hive, Pig, Spark, Datameer and Tableau to support BPQM quality improvement
    needs around analytics, predictive modeling and business intelligence
  * Discover insights and identify opportunities through the use of
    statistical, data mining and visualization techniques
  * Utilize ETL (Extract, Transfer and Load) skills to work with different
    sources of data and creates ways to extract data from sources, as well as,
    import it and modify it to fit the needs of the organization and then add
    it into a data warehouse
  * Conduct training and educational programs in data science
  * Document findings, including assumptions and validation work in a clear
    structured manner
  * Communicate and present findings, orally and visually in a way that can be
    easily understood by their business counterparts
  * Summarize and present findings at executive level meeting and strategy
    sessions
  * Adjust the content and level of detail to appropriate level of the audience
  * Use strong business, problem solving skills and programming knowledge to
    quickly cycle hypotheses through discovery
  * Work closely with other business and technology leaders to frame problem
    definition and potential solutions
  * Create automated anomaly detection systems and constant tracking of
    performance 
  * Develop solutions for key business initiatives ensuring alignment with
    future state analytics vision
  * Engage constructively with project teams to support project objectives
    through the application of sound advanced analytical principles
  * Utilize statistical, mathematical, predictive modeling as well as business
    strategy skills to build the algorithms necessary to ask the right
    questions and find the right answers
  * Designs experiments, test hypotheses, and build models
  * Develop solutions to loosely defined business problems by leveraging
    pattern detection over potentially large datasets
  * Propose innovative ways to look at problems by using data mining
  * Validate findings using an experimental and iterative approach
  * Experience in creating complex queries from scratch through use of SQL
  * Experience in relational database modeling and concepts
  * Experience with ETL processes to extract, transform and load data across
    disparate systems

 



Requirements

To be considered for this position, applicants need to meet the qualifications
listed in this posting.

Required Qualifications:

  * Degree in quantitative field, computer science, statistics, applied
    mathematics, bioinformatics, epidemiology or similar
  * 3+ years of statistical analysis, quantitative analytics, and / or
    forecasting / predicting analytics
  * 2+ years of applying machine learning using distributed systems like
    Hadoop, Hive, Spark or similar libraries
  * Experience working in an Agile Software Development environment
  * Experience with predictive analytics or BI reports within a Hadoop
    ecosystem
  * Experience working on Big Data tools (for example: Hadoop, Hive, Spark,
    Pig, Splunk etc.)
  * Exceptional communication skills and the ability to make complex concepts
    simple and easy to understand

Preferred Qualifications:

  * Experience with R or Python
  * Familiarity with multiple United Healthgroup platforms / data warehouses
