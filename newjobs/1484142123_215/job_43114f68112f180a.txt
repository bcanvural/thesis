CA Technologies provides IT management solutions that help customers manage and
secure complex IT environments to support agile business services.
Organizations of all sizes leverage CA Technologies software and SaaS solutions
to accelerate innovation, transform infrastructure and secure data and
identities, from the data center to the cloud.

 

 

Your future starts here!

We are looking for a talented Data Scientist to join our team. This position
sits within our DevOps business unit based in beautiful Santa Clara, CA. We are
looking for a candidate with a strong work ethic who is adaptable, responsive
and versatile. This position is responsible for leading a few projects in data
analytics to provide significant value and leadership to the projects. This
individual will play an instrumental role in building predictive analytics
capabilities into our suite of products.

 

About the Role:

In this role, you'll help support CA Technologies' charter to transform the IT
industry by:

  * Keeping a pulse on data analytics trends to figure out how CA can tap into
    these trends to execute on multiple projects
  * Understanding CA's business constraints to ensure that data analytics
    strategy proposed in the projects fit with the overall direction of the
    products and is executable.
  * Communicating with scientists as well as engineers.
  * Helping interpret project results to sales / product management as well as
    customers.
  * Bringing about significant innovation and solving complex problems in
    projects based on analytics
  * Performing unit/module testing of software to find errors and confirm
    programs meet specifications.
  * Fixing bugs, adding enhancements, and converting software to other
    platforms.
  * Assisting quality assurance team by providing assistance to testers and
    support personnel as needed to determine system problems.
  * Collaborating with quality assurance in creation of their test plans and
    participating in reviews.
  * Evaluating impact of software performance, and recommending changes to
    software design team.
  * Writing and maintaining documentation to describe program development,
    logic, coding, testing, changes, and corrections.
  * Providing product content to technical writers.
  * Assisting in strategic research and design as directed.
  * Setting up software configuration control and software development
    environments including program source control to ensure reliable backups
    and source code integrity.
  * Providing input to establish and improve departmental processes and
    procedures.
  * Developing plans outlining steps and time tables for developing programs
    and communicate plans and status to management and other development team
    members.
  * May have indirect reports and manage a small project team.
  * Escalating issues to management as appropriate.
  * Mentoring, training, developing and serving as knowledge resource for less
    experienced Software Engineers and Data professionals.

 

How You?ll Stand Out:

The perfect candidate for this role will have a demonstrated record of success
in positions of increasing responsibility over the course of their career. She/
he will have an outstanding track record and reputation for leadership,
software development and innovation. An ideal background will include: 

  * Typically 7 or more years of experience executing on projects as a lead and
    10 or more years of analytic computing experience.
  * In depth knowledge of data science principles and practices
  * Strong experience leading projects, managing multiple tasks simultaneously
    and efficiently
  * Experience with various operating systems (Windows, Linux, Solaris) and
    database platforms (MS-SQL, MySQL, Oracle)
  * Strong experience working within a Scrum team/Agile environment
  * Experience in coaching and mentoring
  * Knowledge and hands-on experience with fundamental concepts, practices and
    procedures of Computer Science and Software Engineering

 

Preferred Education

Master's or Ph.D. degree or global Equivalent in Computer Science, Statistics,
Mathematics, Engineering or Sciences.
