Data Scientist, Junior

Key Role:

Perform as a data scientist with a focus on computer science to apply knowledge
and expertise with distributed scalable Big Data store, including Accumulo,
Cloudbase, HBase, or Bigtable and expertise with the MapReduce programming
model. Leverage expertise in requirements analysis, installation, integration,
evaluation, enhancement, maintenance, testing, and diagnosis or resolution. Use
statistical methods and leverage knowledge of large data sets to characterize
uncertainty through statistical methods. Leverage knowledge of large data set
analysis and the ability to perform research and ensure study results are
properly interpreted and documented.

Basic Qualifications:

-1+ years of experience with developing software in Java, C, C++, or other high
level languages

-Experience with developing software for UNIX or Linux, including Red Hat,
CentOS, or Ubuntu

-Experience with software integration or testing, including analyzing and
implementing test plans and scripts

-Experience with the design and development of object oriented system

-Experience with developing solutions, integrating, extending, and contributing
to Open-source software

-Experience with version control systems, including Github or Gitlab

-TS/SCI clearance with a polygraph

-BS degree in Computer Science, Physics, Statistics, or Mathematics

Additional Qualifications:

-Experience with deploying applications in a Cloud environment

-Experience with designing and developing automated analytic software,
techniques, and algorithms

-Experience with MapReduce programming

-Experience with the Hadoop Distributed File System (HDFS) and with JSON or
BSON

-Experience with distributed scalable Big Data store or NoSQL, including
Accumulo, Cloudbase, HBase, or Bigtable

-Experience with Apache Spark or Apache Storm

-Experience with Apache Kafka

-Hadoop Certified Developer

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to
meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information; TS/SCI
clearance with polygraph is required.

achieve their missions.  Booz Allen is committed to delivering results that
endure.
