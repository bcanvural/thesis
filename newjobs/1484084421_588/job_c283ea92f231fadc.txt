Date: Jan 2, 2017

Location: Palo Alto, CA, US

Company: SAP

Requisition ID: 122969
Work Area: Software-Development Operations
Expected Travel: 0 - 10%
Career Status: Professional
Employment Type: Regular Full Time

 

COMPANY DESCRIPTION

 

As market leader in enterprise application software, SAP helps companies of all
sizes and industries innovate through simplification. From the back office to
the boardroom, warehouse to storefront, on premise to cloud, desktop to mobile
device ? SAP empowers people and organizations to work together more
efficiently and use business insight more effectively to stay ahead of the
competition. SAP applications and services enable customers to operate
profitably, adapt continuously, and grow sustainably.

 

Who we are:

 The SAP Innovation Center Network is a strategic development entity within
SAP, combining software engineering excellence with thought leadership and
entrepreneurial spirit to create new markets for SAP. Across various focus
clusters and by building on strong teams, we pioneer game-changing solutions in
close collaboration with startups, customers & partners in industry and
research as well as with other SAP units. To ensure a successful go-to-market,
we follow an end-to-end responsibility approach. By exploring unconventional
ideas and developing inspiring proofs of concepts, we push the state-of-the-art
in technology and its applied fields to provide SAP with foresight into future
trends.

At innovation center, we create new growth business for SAP by pioneering new
markets and disruptive technologies. you will have opportunity to work on
complete life cycle of products from ideation, incubation, validation and
scaling.

 

What to expect:

  * A very dynamic, fun and start-up type of environment, with a huge potential
    to learn and work on emerging technologies such as Machine learning, AI,
    Cloud, big-data, AR and VR.
  * If you have entrepreneur spirit and have the passion to bring new ideas to
    market, this would be a perfect team.
  * A very self-sufficient team consisting of UX designers, developers, data
    scientists, operations and Product management. 

 

Your Role

  * As part of an agile team, you will work closely with Product manager,
    developers and designers, and build robust scalable solution leveraging
    start-of-the art data mining and machine learning algorithms.
  * Contribute to multiple projects in different phases, ranging from early
    stage concepts to evolving products.
  * Stay up-to-date and provide thought leadership in the area machine learning
    and data analysis.
  * Work closely with Customers and partners to understand the requirements and
    build robust solutions demonstrating SAP?s capabilities.
  * Contribute to overall architecture of the solutions.

 

Basic Qualifications

  * M.S. or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Statistics,
    Applied Math or equivalent
  * 2+ years of professional work experience in data mining, statistical
    modeling and machine learning
  * Proficient in one or more programming languages such as Python, Java, C/C++
  * Deep hands on experience with relational, in-memory and NoSQL databases
  * Proficient in using one or more statistical modeling and programming tools
    such as R, SPSS and Matlab
  * Experience in building applications on at least one cloud platform such as
    HANA Cloud platform, AWS, GCP, Pivotal and heroku.

 

Preferred Qualifications

  * Experience building applications leveraging HANA in-memory platform.
  * Experience in using Hadoop, Spark, Kafka and Cassandra for data processing.
  * It would be helpful if you can share any code samples from your personal
    repositories on GitHub.
