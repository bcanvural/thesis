IQ Invest in Quality

Bolsa de trabajo > Big Data

Lugar de Trabajo > USA

Sueldo: 35,000 a 40,000

IQ - Invest in Quality is a specialized Talent Attraction firm of IT profiles. 

Our client, is an important company focused on IT Services for different
clients in the USA, If you are looking forward to relocate, this is your
chance. 

Now we are looking for:

DATA SCIENTIST CONSULTANT

BS/MS/PhD in Computer Science or equivalent 
With at least 5 years of experience:


  • Solid background in Mathematics, Statistics and Data Mining.
  • Experience of 3 to 5 years developing and deploying predictive analytics
  • Knowledge in R Language
  • Relational database structures, research methods, machine learning. 
  • Cloud based technologies, Big Data technologies (i.e. Hadoop , HBase,
    Lucene/Solr), analytics packages (i.e. R, Mahout, Matlab, Octave, Weka) 
  • Scripting languages (i.e. Python, Perl), programing languages (i.e. Java, C
    /C++, SQL). 


Role Responsibilities:

  • Uses predictive modeling, statistics, Machine Learning, Data Mining, and
    other data analysis techniques to collect, explore, and extract insights
    from structure and unstructured data. 
  • Develop software, algorithms and applications to apply mathematics to data,
    perform large scale experimentation and build data driven apps to translate
    data into intelligence, solve a variety of business problems and enable
    business strategy. 
  • Assists business with casual inferences & observations with finding
    patterns, relationships in data. Must possess strong understanding of
    internal business segment (stakeholders) and possess strong written and
    communication skills.  

Soft skills required: 

  • Works well in a team, goal oriented. 
  • Strong communication and writing skills. 
  • Very strong problem-solving skills are required for this position.

We offer long term projects in USA and the company perk package is: 

  • Monthly Salary: According on your skills and experience
  • Health Insurance
  • Relocation support
  • Personal Time Off:  10 Days in per year


An opportunity like this doesn't come along every day. So, if you meet all the
criteria please send your resume through this page and we will be in touch for
an interview soon.

We want to meet you!!!

Privacy notice: Invest in Quality S.A. de C.V. cares about your
confidentiality; therefore, we want to share our privacy policy: All
information provided is only with recruiting and selection purposes.
