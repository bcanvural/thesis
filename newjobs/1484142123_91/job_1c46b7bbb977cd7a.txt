As a Team Data Scientist at Eaze, you report to the Head of Data and work as
the primary stakeholder and lead in a department's data work. The departments
with which Team Data Scientists work with are Marketing (Growth Scientist),
Operations (Operations Research Scientist), and Product (Product Scientist).

Skills we're looking for:

      + A passion for analyzing data and working on open ended problems
      + Rock-solid SQL and data analysis skills
      + 2+ years previous experience working on analytics and/or data science
        teams
      + An ability to model, drive, and analyze growth oriented experiments
      + A knack for business and how analytics can empower and drive the
        data-driven decision making process
      + Self-motivated with the ability to work independently
      + Strong communication and organizational skills

Responsibilities:

      + Create dashboards and reports to regularly communicate results and
        monitor key metrics
      + Define key metrics for different members of the organization
      + Generate ideas for exploratory analysis to shape future projects and
        provide recommendations for actions
      + Design experiments and interpret the results to draw detailed and
        actionable conclusions
      + Help advocate for and teach data driven decision making within the
        organization

About Eaze
Eaze (eaze.com) is the easiest, fastest, and most professional way to get
medical marijuana delivered. Eaze has quickly become the #1 technology company
in the fast-growing $6 billion cannabis industry by developing the first
logistics technology and on-demand service that can help dispensaries to 
deliver to qualified patients in < 20 minutes. Eaze has raised over $25M from
investors such as DCM Ventures, Fresh VC, and 500 Startups, and is  one of the
first tech companies to receive large scale institutional investment in the
cannabis industry. Eaze currently works with dispensaries that serve in 80+
California cities, across the Bay Area, parts of Los Angeles County, Orange
County and San Diego County.
