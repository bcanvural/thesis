Title: Data Scientist
Date Posted: 01/09/2017

Job Description:


                                   RESOLVIT
                 Bringing Solutions That Make Business Better

Join Resolvit as a Data Scientist and be part of a creative, forward-thinking
team. Our success at deploying skilled, highly knowledgeable experts has landed
us on the Inc. 5000 list of America’s fastest-growing companies four times –
and we’re just getting started.

As the Data Scientist, you will leverage and drive the overall operational
readiness framework to improve productivity across business functions. You will
support Sales and Services with your business analysis, value case development,
finance, and data analytics skills. You will also work with business users
(product owners) to build requirements, business user stories, acceptance
criteria, business scenarios, and use cases, and work with business functional
teams to drive integrated capability delivery. Additionally, you will:

  • Help develop business cases and outcomes for initiatives, and communicate
    quantified benefits delivered and the status of business outcomes
  • Apply your knowledge of business strategies to coordinate business outcomes
    management with SSO stakeholders
  • Manage business outcomes from inception to deployment, sustaining and
    managing business outcomes data in tools and repositories

What You’ll Need to be Successful:

  • More than 5-10 years of experience in operations and project management
    combined
  • More than 3 years of experience in data science and data mining
  • M.A. or Ph.D in computer science, computer engineering, statistics, math,
    or analytics; B.A./B.S. in engineering and/or business (MBA) is also
    welcome
  • Experience working in large, complex organizations
  • Experience with Big Data technologies including Hadoop, MapReduce, Hive,
    Pig, and Cassandra
  • Experience with R, Python, PHP, Ruby, MATLAB, Java, C++, SQL, SAS, and SPSS
  • Experience with multivariate statistics, including regression, principal
    components analysis, and clustering, data-driven predictive model
    development, and large data sets using Teradata, Oracle, or SQL
  • Experience with business intelligence tools, including BusinessObjects,
    MicroStrategy, and Tableau
  • Proven ability to influence and lead outside of your organization and
    across roles and functions
  • Ability to work well independently or as part of a diverse team
  • Demonstrated take-charge attitude and an enthusiasm for fast-paced,
    changing environment
  • Ability to analyze and interpret data visualizations and develop insights
  • Ability to manage data from various sources and consolidate it accurately
  • Excellent communication skills, including the abilities to be an active
    listener and synthesize high-level information
  • Strong project management and analytical skills, with the ability to create
    innovative, viable solutions
  • Experience with Agile methodologies
  • Strong work ethic and team player mentality, with strong decision-making
    and analytical abilities
  • Ability to manage multiple initiatives in varying stages of the initiative
    life cycle
  • Broad knowledge of business infrastructure
  • Demonstrated track record of managing complex business problems and
    creating viable solutions
  • Breadth of leadership and collaboration skills, as well as excellent verbal
    and written communication skills

Benefits:

At Resolvit, you’ll be given the support you need to grow in your consulting
career.  In addition to opportunities for advancement and ongoing training, we
offer:

  • Medical, dental, and vision insurance
  • Life insurance coverage
  • Long-term and short-term disability coverage
  • 401(k) retirement plan with matching
  • Professional support from a dedicated Business Services Manager
  • Networking opportunities with other consultants in your area

We currently have more than 100 open career opportunities across the country,
so be sure to mention the appropriate Job Code with any correspondence!

About Resolvit:

Resolvit is an international technology consulting firm with industry-leading
customers in the financial services, high tech, manufacturing, retail, life
sciences, and government sectors. Through its partnerships, Resolvit delivers
highly impactful, innovative solutions across five core areas: Infrastructure
Modernization, Application Development Services, Enterprise Data Management &
Analytics, Knowledge & Content Management, and Strategic Staffing.
