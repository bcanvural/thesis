    Position Description:

    CGI is looking for a Big Data Developer/ Data Scientist to join our Federal
    Business Unit in our Lafayette, LA office who will support the Federal
    Regulatory account team. This professional will be an integral part of
    supporting our Regulatory Business Unit s Big Data, Advanced Analytics and
    Visualization practice. The person will be given the opportunity to take a
    leadership role in helping develop staff, lead projects and support
    business development activities. The position will be key in helping shape
    the technical direction of the team.

    Your future duties and responsibilities:

    - Providing technical leadership and oversight on projects
    - Development and review of big data architecture and cloud environments
    - Leading technical solution and design
    - Developing Proof of Concepts and big data/real-time systems

    Required qualifications to be successful in this role:

    - 2+ years of relevant experience and 5+ years overall IT experience
    - Experience administrating big data platforms on cloud environments.
    - Experience developing code on distributed environments using Spark.
    - Bachelor s or Advanced degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering,
    or other technical discipline
    - Development of algorithms using Scala, Python, Java on ML platforms
    - Excellent communication skills are a must (writing, presenting, etc.)

    Desired Skills/Experience:
    - Experience with real-time data processing such as Storm or Spark
    Streaming and developing algorithms against real-time data
    - Experience with geospatial data sets.
    - Understanding of EPA, USDA, DOI, DOT or DOE data and process
    - Certifications in Big Data Analytics, Data Science preferred

    What you can expect from us:

    At CGI, we re a team of builders. We call our employees members because all
    who join CGI are building their own company - one that has grown to 65,000
    professionals located in 40 countries. Founded in 1976, CGI is a leading IT
    and business process services firm committed to helping clients succeed. We
    have the global resources, expertise, stability and dedicated professionals
    needed to achieve results for our clients - and for our members. Come grow
    with us. Learn more at www.cgi.com.

    This is a great opportunity to join a winning team. CGI offers a
    competitive compensation package with opportunities for growth and
    professional development. Benefits for full-time, permanent members start
    on the first day of employment and include a paid time-off program and
    profit participation and stock purchase plans.
