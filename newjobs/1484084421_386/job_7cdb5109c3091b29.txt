Data Scientist

Back to Job Categories

The Gig:

This position reports to the CTO and will work closely with other engineers to
build a world-class platform. A strong candidate will have a proven track
record of success, excellent communication skills, consistent work ethic and a
desire to be a part of one of the fastest growing mobile solutions companies in
the industry.

The Work:

  • Create and refine real-time analytic models to understand conversations and
    behaviors in order to target users with optimal messaging and maximize
    customer lifetime value
  • Design experiments leading to a better understanding of our data and how to
    improve its predictive ability
  • Evaluate new sources of data for our retail clients to improve results of
    their analytic models
  • Develop machine learning models to better process our data

The Requirements:

  • Bachelor's or Master's Degree in relevant field of study
  • 3-5 years of experience working in a business intelligence or data
    analytics capacity
  • Well versed in the application of analytic techniques and predictive
    modeling
  • Practical ability to visualize data, communicate about the data, and
    utilize it effectively
  • Innovative instincts and an entrepreneurial spirit
  • Proven ability to develop high quality software in agile environment
  • Good interpersonal skills and ability to communicate effectively at all
    levels of the organization
  • Collaborative team player with ability to effectively implement creative
    ideas
  • Self-motivated, enthusiastic attitude with a desire to maintain a healthy
    atmosphere on the team
  • Ability to learn quickly, think critically and take necessary decisions for
    business
