Position Title:

External Description:
Equifax has a new career opportunity for a Marketing Data Scientist in our US
analytics team. The Marketing Data Scientist is an expert in marketing data and
analytical solutions, and is able to design and develop advanced analytical
solutions for Equifax customers, provide sales support and analytical expertise
to the analytical sales consulting team, and find new insights within the
vertical.  This position will sit at our Alpharetta office.

Position Description 


  * Work with Business Consultants and clients on the formulation of new
    modeling and other analytical solutions across the marketing space
  * Develop innovative analytical solutions within the vertical
  * Integrate diverse data sources into solutions
  * Research new modeling techniques as appropriate for a specific solution
  * Ensure quality end-to-end deliverables of developed analytical solutions
  * Create detailed documentation outlining design and technical specifications
    of each solution
  * Contribute to process improvement initiatives within entire Analytics group

    Position Requirements


  * 8-10+ years' experience in the marketing, risk, fraud, insurance, or other
    relevant analytics arena with at least 2 years in marketing

  * Strong consultative acumen with responsibility for analytical project
    design as well as presentation of results to external customers

  * Strong knowledge of marketing- specific data and the business problems in
    marketing
  * Ability to clearly present statistical solutions to non-statistical people
  * Ability to create new ideas for statistical solutions to customers business
    issues
  * Experience building logistic and linear regression solutions, knowledge of
    alternative methodologies like machine learning preferred
  * Master's degree or higher in Statistics, Mathematics, Economics or other
    related discipline
  * Experience with SAS, SAS Enterprise Miner, and with Hadoop environment
  * Experience with MS Office (Word, Excel, Access, PowerPoint)
  * Experience with WPS/R/S-Plus/SPSS and other statistical packages a plus
  * Knowledge of optimization and design of experiments techniques a plus
