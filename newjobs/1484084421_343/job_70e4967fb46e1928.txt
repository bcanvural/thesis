More information about this job:

Responsibilities:

This position supports the Managing Senior, Psychometrics in the design,
computation, report generation, and data analyses of assigned educational
assessment projects.

As part of a psychometrics team:
• Assist in all psychometric and test development activities related to
assigned educational assessment projects, for example, test calibration, test
equating, item analysis, and dimensionality and reliability analysis.
• Assist in determining the design, analysis and evaluation of the systems,
processes, and services required to meet or exceed contract requirements.
• Assist with writing proposal sections related to psychometric activities.
• Assist in setting and achieving departmental goals.
• Some travel required.

Qualifications:

Required:
• A Bachelor's degree in mathematics, statistics or related field is required.
• Experience with statistical analysis software, e.g.SPSS
• Proficiency with Microsoft Office suite
• Excellent written and oral communication skills
• Strong organizational skills
• Ability to work effectively as part of a team
Preferred:
• Experience in educational assessment
• Programming experience
• Working knowledge of testing methodologies
• Experience with database structures
• Additional education related to measurement or statistics
