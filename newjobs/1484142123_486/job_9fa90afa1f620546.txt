Dewberry is seeking Data Analyst/ Data Scientist Professional preferably with
experience in geospatial analytics. This position will support a wide array of
projects at national, regional, and local scales. Candidates will have strong
competencies and demonstrated experience using statistical, numerical and
computer application techniques to conduct analyses. Candidates will have
strong skills with proficiency in a range of important tools such as pandas, 
Numpy, matplotlib, SciPy, and SQL. Mines and analyzes complex and unstructured
data sets using advanced statistical methods for use in data driven decision
making.

Responsibilities:

Job Duties:

Performs research, analysis, and modeling on organizational data. Develops and
applies algorithms or models to key business metrics with the goal of improving
operations or answering business questions. Provides findings and analysis for
use in decision making.

This position will mostly focus on advanced data analysis and analytics efforts
in support of several Federal and State contracts. This is an excellent career
opportunity for an individual to join a team of professionals while working in
a multidisciplinary consulting and engineering firm.  The position offers great
potential for professional growth and the opportunity to apply the latest
technology to efforts in climate risk adaptation, water resources analyses, and
resilient design.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  * Support large-scale data collection, processing, cleaning, and verifying
    the data used for analytics
  * Data mining using statistical methods
  * Preparing dashboards for visualizing analytics results
  * Conducting data analyses and packaging results tailored for both technical
    and non-technical audience.
  * Coordinating with the internal and external stakeholders to collect
    requirements, inform analyses, and develop presentations of results.

Required Skills & Required Experience:

Must have experience that demonstrates a strong background in the application
of GIS and skills to execute efficient solutions. The candidate should have
strong organizational skills, as well as the ability to multi-task and work
independently and as a team player.

  * Masters degree or Bachelor?s with 5+ years? experience with Data Science,
    Computer science, operation research or any quantitatively related fields.
  * Experience with numerical and statistical packages like pandas, Numpy,
    SciPy, matplotlib, and R.
  * Experience with data visualization tools like Tableau, Microsoft Power BI,
    D3.js, and Visual.ly
  * Experience in writing complex database queries using SQL
  * Preferred experience in distributed computing frameworks likes MapReduce,
    Spark, Hive, Impala
  * Preferred knowledge of machine learning packages likes Scikit-Learn,
    TensorFlow
  * Preferred experience or knowledge of GIS tools like ArcGIS 10x, Leaflet,
    PostGIS, Fiona
  * Innovation, enthusiasm, and creativity - ability to develop new ideas and
    to chart a path
  * Ability to effectively manage multiple assignments concurrently
  * Communicate complex information using visual tools and reporting to the
    technical and non-technical audience.
