  Role Summary:
  In this role, you will drive Data Governance for the organization that
  supports the data requirements of GE Power including functional and
  regional business users as well as Data Scientists who are using analytics
  to achieve business outcomes including building new customer analytics
  products to increase revenue, driving internal productivity to reduce cost
  and managing risk of our contractual services portfolio.

  Essential Responsibilities:

  As the Sr. Director - Data & Analytics you will:

    • Focus on three main aspects of the Power Data Services organization –
      Governance, Project Management and manage the Financials of the team
    • Define the Data Governance Policy and Standards for the Enterprise Data
      Landscape for GE Power.
    • Implement operating mechanisms for ensuring governance standards
      including metadata management and data lineage - includes the Ingestion
      layer, Primary and Secondary Consumption layers and visualization/
      analytics tools like Tableau.
    • Document the end to end business data landscape aligned with Business
      Processes.
    • Drive consistency in data definitions, sources, consumable models, data
      usage and quality.
    • Identify, track and collate all critical metadata in GE Power into the
      Power Data Lake.
    • Actively participate in strategic and tactical initiatives to ensure
      that a data stewardship mindset is incorporated into all projects
    • Implement off-the-shelf / open source tools for data governance like
      Metadata Management. Alternatively build data governance solutions on
      the Predix platform or use Tableau for reporting/visualizations.
    • Provide Project Management structure and tracking for the different
      projects in the team – including driving Fastworks / Agile approach
      using Scrum tools like Rally and Aha.
    • Manage the Finances of the Power Data Services organization including
      tracking SII, OP Budgets and Actuals working with the Finance
      representatives for the IT organization.




  Qualifications/Requirements:

    • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or related field from an
      accredited college or university
    • Minimum of 10 years experience in a Data Management role including
      running queries and compiling data for analytic
    • Minimum of 5 years experience in data design, data architecture and
      data modeling (both transactional and analytic)
    • Minimum of 3 years experience in Big Data / NoSQL technologies
      including Greenplum and Hadoop (HDFS, MapReduce, Hive, Shark, Spark,
      etc.)


                                                                               
  Desired Characteristics:

    • Experience in data dictionary and metadata management using automated
      tools
    • Proven experience in data integration / data federation projects
    • Experience in data design, data architecture and data modeling (both
      transactional and analytic)
    • Knowledge of data transfer and ingestion technologies including Talend
      and HVR
    • Write complex SQL queries needed to query and analyze data
    • Communicates complex solution concepts in simple terms
    • Quickly learn and adapt the capabilities of new technologies and share
      with the team
    • Strong Oral and written communication skills
    • Ability to build visualizations using Tableau to show insights
    • Good knowledge of Predix Platform. Working knowledge and hand on
      Experience with Restful Webservices, Angular JS and Polymer and other
      web components like HTML5 and CSS
    • Usage of Agile / Scrum tools like Rally and Aha
