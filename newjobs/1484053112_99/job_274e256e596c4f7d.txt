Date: Dec 29, 2016

Location: Greenville, SC, US, 29601

Company: Greenville Health System

Job Class 

NON CLINICAL PROFESSIONAL 

 

FLSA 

Exempt 

 

Job Summary 

Develops financial and clinical data analytics and analysis based on claims
(administrative) data and electronic medical records (EMR) with a focus on
population health outcomes. Supports both business and health outcome related
analysis. Assesses data analytics needs and recommends, executes, analyzes, and
prepares reports to respond to those needs. Works closely with both business
and clinical team(s) to determine the most appropriate and effective analytics
approaches. Utilizes a variety of systems and software tools as needed to
compile and prepare reports, graphs and charts of data analyzed. Develops novel
and innovative approaches to measuring population health impacts and associated
investments. Research and introduces additional data sources and analysis
techniques as needed. Remains current with modern data analysis tools and
methods. Participates in both regular/on-going reporting and analysis as well
as one-time/ad hoc analysis to respond to or analyze a specific business need,
issue or opportunity. Works effectively both independently and as a member of a
team.     

 

Supervisory/Management Responsibilities 

This is a non-management job that will report to a supervisor, manager,
director or executive. 

 

Minimum Education 

Master's Degree in health/healthcare informatics, bioinformatics,
biostatistics, or health economics

 

Minimum Experience 

One year data analytics, statistics, visualization tools

 

Required Certifications/Registrations/Licenses 

NOT APPLICABLE 

 

Specific Acceptable Credentials (if applicable) 

Not Applicable

 

In lieu of the Above Minimum Requirements 

Not Applicable

 

Other Required Experience 

Experience with health/healthcare data/data analytics - Required

Experience with modern data analysis/analytics tools such as statistical
packages (SAS, SPSS, R), visualization tools (Tableau, qlik, Matlab) and/or
other data analysis tools  - Required

Experience and knowledge of health care risk stratification methodologies and
experience with risk stratification tools such as Milliman's MARA and
MedInsight, LexisNexis MEDai or other health care risk stratification tools and
methodologies - Preferred   

Experience with health care grouper methodologies such as CMS' Heirarchical
Condition Categories (HCC), Optum's Episode Treatment Grouper (ETG), and/or
Health Cost Guidelines (HCG). Experience with episode payment models (bundles)
- Preferred     

    

 

Position Posting Category 

Non Clinial Professional/Technical

 


Nearest Major Market: Greenville
Nearest Secondary Market: South Carolina
Job Segment: Database, Medical, Developer, Informatics, Data Analyst,
Technology, Healthcare, Data
