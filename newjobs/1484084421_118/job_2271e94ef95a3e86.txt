Date: Jan 9, 2017

Location: LAKE FOREST, IL, US, 60045-5202

Company: Grainger

Major Purpose:

Apply statistical and mathematical techniques to the ongoing pursuit of
“understanding the customer”

 

 

Position Description:

 

  • Use state-of-the-art analytical skills and techniques, both technical and
    intuitive to analyze and model customer behavior allowing recommendations
    that drive marketing and sales decisions. Provide Marketing with superior
    analytical expertise.

  • Development and maintenance of statistical models in support of internal
    client activities
  • Plan, execute, and effectively communicate the results of strategic
    database analyses
  • Conducts analysis of market data and customer information, identifying
    patterns, trends and translating those into actionable insights
  • Utilize measurement and analysis techniques to quantify results of business
    activities. Assist in the education of business
  • partners on measurement and analysis methodologies
  • Research and recommend innovative solutions to a wide array of business
    problems using statistics and mathematics

 

 

Position Requirements:

 

  • Bachelor’s degree in statistics, economics, mathematics, industrial
    engineering or related field. Masters or 2 years
  • Equivalent work experience preferred.
  • Expert with linear regression, logistic regression, cluster analysis, and
    time series analysis
  • Strong business acumen
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Intellectual curiosity

 

 

Impact on the Organization:

 

  • The decisions made by this position have a profound impact on the
    organization through the development of analytical work that influences
    overall company strategy and enhances the success of tactical initiatives

 

 

“Grainger is an Equal Opportunity / Affirmative Action employer. All qualified
applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race,
color, religion, sex, national origin, gender identity, sexual orientation,
disability, or protected veteran status.”


Job Segment: Database, Supply, Supply Chain, Technology, Operations
