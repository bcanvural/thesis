Senior Software Engineer - Data Scientist

  • Irving, TX - United States
  • Research - Full-Time
  • Job # 444477
  • Date posted - 01-06-2017

Apply Share this Job

Responsibilities

We are seeking innovative software engineer/data scientist to be part of a
small team focused on building new product prototypes utilizing machine
learning/artificial intelligence.   In this role, you will be responsible for
training and fine-tuning state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms and
applying them in a scalable manner.  You will also use your programming skills
to integrate these models into a functioning user friendly prototype for
demonstration of the business potential to internal/external partners. 

We operate with a lean startup mentality developing early prototypes in 6-8
week iterative development cycles often using open-source tools.  In this role,
you should have a real passion for understanding and advancing new emerging
technologies and applying that knowledge back to the community, advancing
intellectual property or internal Verizon innovation pipeline.  

Qualifications

Must Have:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Statistics, Math, Computer Science, Data Science or a
    related field or six or more years of equivalent work experience
  • Knowledge of software programming languages (e.g. Java, JavaScript/Node.js,
    Python, Swift) to apply to development of a prototype. 

 

 Ideally, you will also have:

  • Master’s Degree in Statistics, Math, Computer Science, DataScience or a
    related field.
  • Capability to self-learn new technologies in 4-6 week timeframe (e.g.
    Programming languages, machine learning open source models, etc)
  • Knowledge of docker container environments and linux systems
  • Data Science:  Machine Learning Algorithms  and Frameworks (Torch, Caffe or
    CNTK)

Not to boast, but a little bit about us

Verizon powers America’s fastest and most reliable network. We’re also leading
the way in cloud and security solutions, Internet of Things and video
entertainment. Technology moves fast and so do we. We believe that bringing
great ideas and customer experiences to life should be recognized and
rewarded.  Whether you think in code, words, pictures or numbers, find your
future at Verizon.
