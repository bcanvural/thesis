Data Scientist

29.12.2016 NYC New York

Dailymotion is one of the world?s leading video-sharing platforms. It attracts
over 300 million unique visitors and 3 billion videos views worldwide per month

premium partners.
As part of its growing activities, Dailymotion is looking to enforce its

Job description

- Formulate approaches to solve problems using well-defined algorithms and data
sources.
- Incorporate an understanding of product functionality and customer
perspective to provide context for those problems.
- Use data exploration techniques to discover new questions or opportunities
within your problem area
- Contribute to delivering innovations in the data science space.
- Interpret the results of your analysis, validate the approach, and learn to
monitor, analyze, and iterate to continuously improve.

Job Requirements

Relevant degree in computer science, statistics or mathematics
Engineering experience using big data systems on SQL, Hadoop, etc.
Familiarity with Hive, Pig, Spark, and Storm is a plus.Proficiency in SQL, R or
Python, with demonstrable depth of coding skills
Experience performing data analysis and applying statistics concepts using
tools such as: Excel, R, MATLAB/Simulink, AMPL, or SAS/JMP.
Understanding machine learning principles and limitations is a plus.
Passion to learn how to be an excellent Data Scientist from your peers,
manager, and other stakeholders.
Ability to interact with peers and stakeholders to drive product and business
impact.
Strong interpersonal and communications skills
Good level of fluency in English (spoken, written)
