Business Intelligence Analyst

Apply Company The Boeing Company Job ID 1600021822 Date posted 12/21/2016
Location Renton Washington United States
Job Description
We are seeking a Business Intelligence Analyst as a member of our MS Analytics
Data and Infrastructure team. You will have the opportunity to make significant
contributions to the financial performance of Commercial Aviation Services.

This role will support the Data and Infrastructure Analytics team tasked with
developing the analytics infrastructure needs for the BU and assimilating
existing and new data sets into ADW as readily usable views.

Other responsibilities include:

- Lead and perform preparation of highly complex data for analysis, e.g.
cleansing, conditioning, transforming, filling in missing fields, gathering or
derivation of new feature variables and handling complexities and correlations
in multi-variate data.

- Organize data for analysis appropriate for the volume of structured and
unstructured data.

- Determine best practices for preparation of data for analytic requirements.

- Work with business partners on incorporating analytics into their processes.

- Determine stakeholder needs and ensure business value is realized.

- Communicate, advise and collaborate with business partners.

- Manage and monitor production and deployment. Lead cross-functional
deployment teams to deliver decision support visualizations and reports,
algorithms, models, dashboards and/or tools.
Boeing is the world's largest aerospace company and leading manufacturer of
commercial airplanes and defense, space and security systems. We are engineers
and technicians. Skilled scientists and thinkers. Bold innovators and dreamers.
Join us, and you can build something better for yourself, for our customers and
for the world.

Employer will not sponsor applicants for employment visa status

Qualifications
Technical bachelor's degree and typically 9 or more years' related work
experience or a Master's degree with typically 7 or more years' or a PhD degree
with typically 4 or more years' related work experience.  Preference is for a
technical bachelor?s degree which is defined as any four year degree, or
greater, in a mathematic, scientific or information technology field of study.

This position requires:

- Hands on experience developing data objects ie:  RDBMS
- Experience implementing ERP systems (SAP/Oracle/Lawson, BaaN)
- Experience working with databases and interpreting data
- Experience with business case analysis (problem identification, quantitative
modeling and problem solving)

Desired Experience, Skills and Qualification:

- Data visualizations experience
- Very strong supply chain principles, retail and aerospace experience
preferred
