Mathematical Statistician

Apply Now

Job ID 3720088 Date posted 01/04/2017
Department: HHS CDC Center
Agency: Centers for Disease Control and Prevention
Job Announcement Number: HHS-CDC-D3-17-1855421
SALARY RANGE: $106380 to $138296/Per Year
OPEN PERIOD: 2017-01-04 to 2017-01-12
SERIES & GRADE: GS-1529-14/14
POSITION INFORMATION: Full Time
WHO MAY BE CONSIDERED: United States Citizens

AGENCY MARKETING STATEMENT:
What exciting career opportunities await you at CDC?

JOB SUMMARY:

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) is the agency Americans
trust with their lives. As a global leader in public health, CDC is the
nation's premier health promotion, prevention, and preparedness agency. Whether
we are protecting the American people from public health threats, researching
emerging diseases, or mobilizing public health programs with our domestic and
international partners, we rely on our employees to make a real difference in
the health and well-being of people here and around the world.

Who May Apply: Open to US citizens and applicants eligible under Schedule A
authority.

This vacancy is also being announced concurrently with vacancy announcement
HHS-CDC-M3-17-1855519 under merit promotion procedures. Please review that
announcement to see if you are eligible for consideration under merit promotion
procedures. NOTE: Applicants must apply separately for each announcement in
order to be considered.

This position is located in the Department of Health and Human Services (DHHS),
Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), National Center for Emerging
Zoonotic Infectious Diseases (NCEZID), Division of Food, Waterborne, and
Environmental Diseases (DFWED), Office of the Director (OD), in Atlanta,
Georgia.

PHS Commissioned Corps Officers interested in performing the duties of this
position within the Commissioned Corps (not as a career/career-conditional
employee) are encouraged to apply under the merit promotion announcement
indicated above.

Additional selections may be made within the same geographical location
CDC-wide.

CDC is an Equal Opportunity Employer.



KEY REQUIREMENTS: Transcripts are required.


DUTIES:

As a Mathematical Statistician you will: 

  * Plan and lead primary research studies or surveillance efforts 
  * Plan and lead secondary research studies 
  * Provide leadership in the design, development, and maintenance of publicly
    accessible disease-related and injury-related query and reporting
    applications 
  * Lead or carry out comparative reviews of statistical methods and
    statistical analysis software, and make recommendations regarding the most
    appropriate choices in various research and analysis scenarios 
  * Provide statistical consulting 
  * Extend established statistical methods and software and develop new
    approaches to meet novel or emerging data analysis or research needs 
  * Write reports based on the results of primary or secondary research
    studies, and disseminate results through publications and presentations



QUALIFICATIONS REQUIRED:

BASIC REQUIREMENTS:
A. A full four-year course of study at an accredited college or university
leading to a bachelor's or higher degree that includes 24 semester hours of
mathematics and statistics, of which at least 12 semester hours were in
mathematics and 6 semester hours were in statistics.
                                                                                              
-OR-
B. A combination of education and experience with at least 24 semester hours of
mathematics and statistics, including 12 semester hours were in mathematics and
6 semester hours were in statistics, as shown in A above, plus appropriate
experience or additional education.
MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:
In addition to meeting the basic requirements above, applicants must have at
least one year of specialized experience at or equivalent to the GS-13 grade
level in the Federal service as defined in the next paragraph:
Specialized experience is experience which is directly related to the position
which has equipped the applicant with the particular knowledge, skills and
abilities (KSAs) to successfully perform the duties of the position in
providing advice and consultation on mathematics, statistics, research methods
and data management, planning, developing, evaluating and executing
mathematical statistical approaches for research, surveillance systems, and
other studies, and facilitating data collection and analysis activities related
to epidemiology, laboratory science, and bioinformatics to ensure the
appropriate application of mathematical statistical methods.



HOW YOU WILL BE EVALUATED:

The category assignment is a measure of the degree to which your background
matches the competencies required for this position. Qualified candidates will
be ranked into one of three categories: Best Qualified, Well Qualified or
Qualified. The Category Rating Process does not add veterans  preference points
but protects the rights of veterans by placing them ahead of non-preference
eligibles within each category. Preference eligibles who meet the minimum
qualification requirements and who have a compensable service-connected
disability of at least 10 percent must be listed in the highest quality
category (except in the case of scientific or professional positions at the
GS-9 level or higher). Your qualifications will be evaluated on the following
competencies (knowledge, skills, abilities and other characteristics). You do
not have to respond to the KSAs separately but your resume should contain
sufficient information to demonstrate possession of the KSAs.

 1. Model a high standard of quality and commitment to organizational
    objectives
 2. Provide expert advice, methodologies and problem-solving assistance to
    customers 
 3. Determine the methodological and statistical approach for applied health
    research 
 4. Use a variety of approaches to manage and resolve concerns, disagreements,
    and conflicts
 5. Build professional skills and competencies and improve work processes 
 6. Foster and initiate new ideas, methods and solutions 
 7. Commit to satisfying internal and external customers 
 8. Identify and apply analytical methods, techniques, and tools to analyze and
    interpret data 
 9. Collaborate with investigators to facilitate data collection 
10. Assess data for completeness, accuracy, and quality 
11. Organize, store, secure, document, and manage data to facilitate
    appropriate access and use
12. Make decisions in a timely manner 
13. Recognize each person's differences and utilize those to increase
    organizational effectiveness 
14. Respond quickly to change and easily consider new approaches 
15. Gain the trust of others by taking responsibility for own actions and
    telling the truth 
16. Consider and respond appropriately to the needs and feelings of people in
    different situations 
17. Foster an inclusive workplace where differences are valued and used to
    achieve the mission 
18. Deliver clear, effective communication and take responsibility for
    understanding others 
19. Monitor and evaluate performance against goals, and makes adjustments to
    achieve goals 
20. Accurately assess problems and effectively and efficiently arrive at
    effective solutions 
21. Create and maintain an environment that guides a project to its successful
    completion 
22. Apply knowledge of public health principles, theory & policy to improve
    public health approaches 
23. Apply a comprehensive and evolving knowledge of statistical theory, methods
    and techniques
24. Present and disseminate results of statistical analyses to target audiences
25. Respond professionally in stressful and difficult situations 
26. Focus on desired results and set and achieve challenging goals 
27. Proactively identify and act on problems and opportunities
28. Software Literacy] Apply appropriate statistical software packages based on
    their capabilities and limitations
29. Provide direction and leadership to help teams achieve goals & operate
    cooperatively & cohesively
30. Demonstrate a depth of judgment, knowledge and technical skill important
    for the position
31. Express oneself clearly in business writing
