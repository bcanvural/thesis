Description

Brief Summary

The Data Scientist (Operations Engineering) will be responsible for production
modeling, financial analysis, module vendor comparison, comparative analysis,
and informing PV system design of SoCore’s nationwide solar PV portfolio.  This
person will be in direct contact with the design, procurement and field
operations teams to inform system design, vendor selection and production
monitoring.  The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in engineering
and experience with computer programming and data analysis within the energy
space.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform production estimates for solar PV projects using production
    estimation software such as PVSYST, PVWATTS, SAM, and Helioscope
  • Prepare reports and comparisons between system configurations, module
    vendors, and multiple variables simultaneously for project optimization
  • Remain up-to-date on new technologies, trends, and industry standards for
    production modeling to ensure installations are compliant with industry
    best practices and prudent assumptions within the PV energy market
  • Monitors historical production of existing project installations to verify
    performance in accordance with financial expectations and address any
    discrepancies in the PVSYST models compared to actual system production
    over time
  • Reports production and O&M activity across departments
  • Troubleshoots system issues and helps investigate root cause of frequent
    failures
  • Performs system acceptance testing at the completion of project

Core Competencies

  • Drives results
  • Thinks conceptually
  • Self Manages
  • Takes initiative and ownership of projects
  • Solves problems
  • Communicates effectively (written & verbal)

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering - or comparable technical
    knowledge through demonstrated work experience; Master’s degree is strongly
    preferred
  • Database Administration experience
  • Experience with scripting languages including SQL,Python and JAVA
  • Solid understanding of power generation plants, utility systems, and
    photovoltaic systems
  • Experience with production estimating software tools such as PVSYST,
    PVWATTS, SAM, Helioscope
  • Direct experience working with Field Service Engineers
  • 5-7 years of experience in a similar position, ideally in the renewable
    energy industry and in utility environment

Additional Notes

  • Must hold a valid driver’s license
  • Travel required (up to 25%)
  • Physically able to climb a ladder in excess of 30 feet and work at
    elevation on roof for extended periods of time in hot or cold weather
    conditions.
