Description

As a subject matter expert in the analytics domain, you will be designing and
building complex models that learn from Infoblox networks and DNS data to
provide security solutions. We are seeking professionals who are well versed in
scalable data mining, machine learning techniques, and love to build analytics
models. The successful candidate will be working in a fast-paced,
geographically diverse, multidisciplinary environment and a competitive
landscape where new data flows in rapidly and the world is constantly changing.

This is a key position in the CTO organization with very high visibility to
upper management.  You will have the opportunity to work with experts of next
generation technology and help drive the project and product portfolio to
achieve the departmental and corporate goals.  This position is located in
Tacoma, WA.

Responsibilities:

  • Apply data-mining and machine learning techniques for a variety of modeling
    and relevance problems involving DNS and networking data
  • Build complex statistical models that learn from and scale to petabytes of
    data
  • Work with architects and product managers to identify analytics initiatives
    and develop efficient and reliable algorithms
  • Work collaboratively across multi-disciplinary teams throughout the company
    as a subject matter expert in analytics with a focus on security solutions
    and implementation

Requirements:

  • Experience in software development working with big data and analytics
    products
  • Expertise in data mining, machine learning, statistical models, data
    clustering and classification techniques
  • Experience in architecting/designing large and complex data analytics
    including data modeling and feature extraction
  • This is a hands-on position, must be able to write code individually or
    part of a team
  • Full software development life-cycle experience, with proven track record
    of shipping product

Preferred Experience:

  • Expertise in streaming analytics frameworks such as Apache Spark,
    STORM, MOA and WekA
  • Knowledge of Hadoop architecture, HDFS, Map-Reduce Algorithms and Elastic
    Search
  • Understanding of distributed system and clustering architectures – massive
    grid scale performance with reliability
  • Network security background, familiarity with botnets, DDoS,
  • Experience with SQL and NoSQL(e.g. Cassandra) and data visualization
    techniques

Education

  • B.S. in CS, CE or EE is required
  • Masters or PhD in CS, CE or EE is preferred
