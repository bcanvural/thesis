Engineer/Software Developer (Dayton, OH)

OH-Dayton

Category: Information Technology
Schedule: Full-Time

PLEASE NOTE THAT THIS IS A CONTINGENT POSITION

The incumbent provides software development support to QUASAR Task Order 51,
RPA Operator Color Vision requirements Research and Operational Vision
Assessment (OBVA) Research Support.  This effort supports USAFSAM’s Operational
Based Vision Assessment (OBVA) research involving human subject data
collection.  This effort will leverage OBVA research facilities, newly
developed computer-based vision testing capabilities, and personnel.  The
purpose of the OBVA program is to investigate and validate the relationships
between operational visual performance and visual standards, proposed future
tests, and other measure.  Incumbent will perform other duties and assignments
as required.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Provides software development to support research objectives of QUASAR Task
    Order 51
  • Provides technical support to the research scientist and other task order
    leadership
  • Provides troubleshooting and support as needed
  • Provides collaboration with vendors to ensure technological issues are
    addressed and resolved to meet contract deliverables and timelines
  • Provides inputs to research objectives and reports as required
  • Ensures compliance with contract requirements, USAF Instructions and
    KBRwyle company policies and procedures 

Required Education/Experience:  

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering or relevant
    field
  • 2-4 years’ experience in software, software development and technical
    research
  • 3D Graphics experience

Required Skills/Training:

  • Experience working with Image Generator (IG) software
  • Experience with C++, C# or Visual Basic
  • Experience with Matlab

Desired Education, Experience, Skills or Training:

  • Experience supporting research activities
  • Experience working with Department of Defense on Federal contracts
  • Experience working with visual and color vision-based research

Physical & Cognitive Requirements:

• Organization: Prioritize multiple, competing assignments.

• Hearing: Must be able to hear well enough to communicate with co-workers.

• Must be able to stand, walk, climb, stoop, kneel, lift, pull and push.
• Travel Requirement: Must be willing to travel as necessary to meet business
needs.
