Senior/Lead Data Scientist, Community IQ Team
Locations: Seattle or San Francisco

Salesforce is looking for an exceptional data scientist who will help shape and
drive the future of Salesforce?s communities platform that connects millions of
people across organizations around the world enabling them to be more
productive and collaborate more effectively. You will be embedded in a
cross-functional team of talented technologists who all share a passion for
writing clean code, designing well thought-out APIs; and designing scalable,
data-driven, well-designed and well-tested products and systems.

Required skills:

  * At least 5+ years of hands-on data science experience working with applied
    data driven teams and track record of conceiving and delivering important
    data products and being a top performer in current and past roles
  * Excellent proficiency in Python, Java/C++, R
  * Experience working with large datasets, preferably using tools like Hadoop,
    Spark.
  * Ability to collaborate with and synthesize input from technical and
    non-technical stakeholders to build a shared vision and roadmap across
    Product and Technology
  * Strong understanding of Machine learning technologies, statistical
    concepts, advanced algorithms, data structures, etc.
  * Comfortable with data analysis and visualization using tools like Scipy, R,
    Matlab, Spark, etc.
  * Strong familiarity with relational database or NoSQL technologies, queries,
    and schema design
  * Advanced degree in computer science or related discipline.

Desired Skills:

  * Ph.D. in computer science or related discipline.
  * Work with a self-organizing team that values team excellence and
    productivity
  * Have a passion for quality and writing clean and robust well-instrumented
    code that scales and performs well
  * Adopt, embrace, and promote data-driven practices with your peers in an
    agile environment
  * Published work in data mining, machine learning, or related conferences
  * Comfortable presenting the results of experiments to variety of audiences.

Academic and practical knowledge in some of the following areas:

  * Information retrieval
  * Machine Learning (ML)
  * Recommendation Systems
  * Natural Language Processing
  * Controlled experimentation (A/B testing)
  * Deep learning technologies

?Example Problems:

  * Use most recent advances in natural language processing technology to
    develop a smart question and answering platform.
  * Solve learning to rank problems for feeds that can scale up across many
    different types of organization.
  * Create virtual assistants that can help customers, associates and partners
    of organizations.
