UserTesting

Data Engineer

  • Mountain View, CA
  • Full-time

Company Description

We&apos;re a pre-IPO company with campuses in Mountain View, San Francisco, and
Atlanta. We make it easy for businesses to understand how customers interact
with their digital properties so that they can improve their customer
experience. We do this by giving companies on-demand access to people in their
exact target market who deliver video, audio, and written feedback on websites,
mobile apps, and prototypes. Over 34,000 companies, including Google, Facebook,
CBS, Apple, and The Home Depot, use UserTesting to improve their user
experience—and their bottom line.

Job Description

As a Data Engineer, you will design and and grow a data infrastructure that
powers our ability to make timely, data-driven decisions to fuel and accelerate
the growth of our business.

Responsibilities:

  • Design, build, and maintain data warehouse infrastructure, data structures
    and data pipelines to enable fast and accurate reporting and analysis
  • Work across product and engineering to consume tracking data and developing
    a flexible data model for unstructured data
  • Partner with other analytics team members to collect and enrich data to
    generate insights from disparate sources
  • Work with data scientists and engineers to leverage Machine Learning into
    product features
  • Build out advanced analytics capabilities including data visualization and
    experimentation platforms and tools

Qualifications

Must have:

  • 3+ years experience with relational database system(s), with expertise in
    SQL
  • 3+ years of data warehousing experience
  • 3+ years programming experience in Python, Ruby, or Java
  • Experience with ETL management tools such as: Pentaho Kettle, Azkaban,
    Airflow, or similar
  • Experience in Agile methodologies

Nice to have:

  • Experience with data processing and scalable systems including: Redshift,
    Hadoop, Pig, Hive, etc.
  • Familiarity with streaming technologies: Kafka, AWS Kinesis
  • Familiarity with Business Intelligence tools and platforms: SFDC, Marketo,
    Google Analytics, Optimizely, Tableau, Periscope
  • Familiarity with Machine Learning: TensorFlow, NumPy, Scikit-Learn, Mahout,
    etc.

Additional Information

Besides a great work environment and the opportunity to change the world, we
offer competitive salary, benefits, plenty of perks, as well as stock options.
