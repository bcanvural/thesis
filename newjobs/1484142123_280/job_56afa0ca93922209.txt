Job Title: Health Data Scientist - Data Informatics & Analytics
Location: Map II
City, State: Newark, Delaware

General Description:

This Healthcare Data Scientist provides analytical leadership and support to
ensure that the service lines are optimized to improve outcomes. In addition to
analysis of outcome measure reporting, responsibilities include supporting
quality, patient safety and process improvement projects which align with the
organization?s strategies for optimal health, exceptional experience and
organizational vitality.

Requirements:
? Active nursing license in the state of Delaware required
? Master?s degree in nursing informatics, or other applicable healthcare field
required
? Requires 5 years of experience in working with clinical data for statistical
analysis
? Must have analytical ability to solve routine business and technical
problems.
? Expertise with technologies such as R, SAS, SPSS, Tableau, Excel, Minitab,
Power Insight, Qlikview, is preferred.
? Expertise in using electronic medical records, Microsoft applications (Excel,
Word, PowerPoint, Access, Visio)
? Demonstrated achievement leading transformational organizational projects for
quality and/or safety.
? Certification by National Association for Health Care Quality (CPHQ) required
within six months of hire
? Lean Six Sigma Green Belt required; Lean Six Sigma Black Belt preferred
? Experience in successfully leading, facilitating and participating in
multi-disciplinary teams using performance improvement tools and methodologies
required


Work Shift: Day shift, No Rotation
Position Type: Nursing
