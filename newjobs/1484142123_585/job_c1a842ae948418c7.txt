Research Scientist

Submit to Job(s)    
Req ID :
376012BR_2
Job Code/Title :
E1482:Research Scientist
Job Description :
The individual selected for this Lockheed Martin position will function as both
a practicing Data Scientist and an evangelist for advanced analytics
techniques, primarily in support of the HR Workforce Strategy domain area.

The position's responsibilities include:
-collaboration with business stakeholders to apply and adapt existing models to
new scenarios
-identify business opportunities and needs to develop associated analytical
models that deliver useful, actionable insights, or recommendations.
-participate in, or lead cross functional teams to address problem
-identify new data driven business opportunities beyond the current business
focus by combining existing and new data and help to institutionalize a
data-driven analysis
-identifying relevant data sources and applying a variety of tools and
techniques to prepare and manipulate the data for analysis
-visualization and reporting techniques to present findings and recommendations
-drive data architectures needed for sourcing and wider consumption and
analysis in addition to applied problem solving
-collaborating with Solution Architects to provide insights into current and
future trends
-translating trends into implications for Lockheed Martin including deployment
of new technologies
-giving advice on adequate quality, analysis, processing, privacy and security
of internal and external data
-helping to improve our predictive and cognitive capabilities.

This position requires self-motivated individuals combined with hands-on
entrepreneurial spirit and exceptional drive, with the ability to work in a
fast-paced multi-disciplinary environment.

Limited travel (less than 25%) will be required for this role.

Work location is required to be at a major Lockheed Martin EBS facility. Either
Ft Worth, TX, King of Prussia, PA or Bethesda, MD is preferred.

Basic Qualifications
? Demonstrated strong proficiency in designing efficient algorithms and
performing analysis with a numerical scripting language such as R, MATLAB or
Python
? Strong working knowledge of basic statistical concepts such as point
estimation, probability distributions, hypothesis tests, and how they can be
applied.
? Strong understanding of business fundamentals and the ability to relate them
to business processes
? Ability to utilize flexible analytical approaches that allows for results at
varying levels of precision
? Demonstrated business and technical communication skills, including the
communication of findings using reporting and visualization tools and their
relevance in business terms
? Experience with integration and cleansing of disparate or incomplete data
sets to deliver insight
? Proficiency with SQL

Desired skills
? Experience in design and analysis of simulation experiments
? Experience in applied machine learning, such as generalized linear, decision
tree, and neural network models
? Ability to perform model selection and evaluation using criteria such as Root
Mean Square Error, Receiver Operator Characteristics or Lift tests
? Comfort manipulating and analyzing complex, high-volume, high-dimensionality
data from varying sources combined with a strong passion for empirical research
and for answering hard questions with data and data models
? Experience with SAP HANA, Hadoop (including Hive, Pig or Mahout) and other
tools that facilitate the consumption, storage, and analysis of ?Big Data?
? An understanding of traditional business intelligence and data warehousing
concepts (ETL, data modeling, and reporting)
? Domain knowledge of Aerospace & Defense industry
? Experience designing efficient Data mining and text mining frameworks with
related tools (e.g. Mahout, Weka, UIMA, GATE)
? Project Management experience planning and monitoring analytics projects
using Agile/Scrum principles

As a leading technology innovation company, Lockheed Martin?s vast team works
with partners around the world to bring proven performance to our customers?
toughest challenges. Lockheed Martin has employees based in many states
throughout the U.S., and Internationally, with business locations in many
nations and territories.

Join us at Lockheed Martin, where we?re engineering a better tomorrow.
