Company Description

Gravity is a highly collaborative environment where our culture is to learn,
teach and share. We maintain an energetic social atmosphere where lunch,
punning and kitten wrangling list amongst the things we enjoy most, as well as
hosting regular game nights and happy hour. Due to our location on Main St we
also enjoy food trucks, beach volleyball and regular surf outings! We&apos;re a
very tech savvy bunch. We particularly like to experiment with the latest and
greatest tech and we&apos;re very proud of the approach we have to solving some
of the big-data and scale challenges that we work with every day.

Gravity is looking for someone with a strong work ethic who thrives in a
startup environment like ours. We particularly like self-starters who
demonstrate product ownership and take on responsibility and accountability.

Job Description

As part of a small but highly capable team you will be contributing to our
Hadoop based recommendation systems in Scala (Java experience required) and
will:

  • Take ownership of data flows and maintain responsibility for your code
    throughout the software lifecycle
  • Contribute to the evolution of our algorithm tuner, a machine learning
    pipeline
  • Work in and contribute to a shared codebase
  • Participate in design and code reviews
  • Rapidly fix bugs and solve problems
  • Integrate with existing systems and tools

Qualifications

  • Experience with machine learning models, specifically reinforcement
    learning and bayesian networks
  • Experience with Spark
  • Experience with Hbase
  • Experience with various input/output file formats, i.e. Parquet 
  • Experience with multi-cluster environments
  • A disciplined approach to testing, quality assurance, and operational
    concerns
  • Experience with MapReduce, Impala, and/or Big Data infrastructures
  • 5+ years of industry experience
