Dailymotion is one of the world?s leading video-sharing platforms. It attracts

Job description

As the Director of Data Science, you will:
Lead, hire, manage and mentor a global team of Data Scientists.
Working with fast-paced initiatives, overseeing projects from idea conception
and strategy development to deployment, production, and operations.
Review, direct, guide the analytical work of entire team
Processing, cleansing, and verifying the integrity of the data used for
analysis
Selecting and engineering features to build and optimize prediction and
classification systems
Data mining using state-of-the-art methods
Enhancing data collection procedures to include information that is relevant
for building analytic systems

Job Requirements

A Masters or PhD in computer science, statistics or a related field
(Mathematics, Physics?)
Understanding of online advertising technologies and ecosystem (Ad-Tech
Background)
Predictive modelling experience
Management experience
Excellent understanding and experience of machine learning techniques (eg k-NN,
Naive Bayes, SVM, Decision Forests, Neural Networks, etc.)
Experience with common data science toolkits, such as R, NLP, Weka, NumPy,
MatLab.
Proficiency in at least one high level programming language like Java, Scala,
or Python
Excellence in at least one of these is highly desirable
Great business sense and communication skills
Proficiency in using query languages (SQL, Hive, Pig?) and in MySQL or NoSQL
databases (MongoDB, Cassandra, HBase)
Consultative nature, with a proven track record of using data to provide
actionable business results
Data-oriented personality
