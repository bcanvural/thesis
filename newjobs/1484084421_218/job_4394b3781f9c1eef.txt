About Finicity

Finicity is a leading Financial Data Aggregator and financial technology
company with a wealth of fascinating business opportunities and some of
the world’s most interesting data science challenges. 

We have built a culture that centers around enabling and celebrating the
financial success of our customers. We see a future where financial account
data is delivered instantly to account holders in any app they choose. We
believe that secure data access, high quality data, and intelligent data
enrichment are core to unlocking financial application innovation that will
help consumers and the financial marketplace make better financial decisions.
We believe that better financial decision making will make the world a better
place.

Our mission is 3-fold:

 1. Democratize Financial Event Data – To free the data created during a
    financial account event and make it available to account owners in any
    application they choose.
 2. Improve Financial Data Access Security – To harden the security around the
    access and delivery of financial account data to account owners through
    industry best practices and standards.
 3. Unlock Financial Application Innovation – To improve account owners
    financial outcomes within the financial apps they choose.

Job Description

Finicitys Data Innovation Lab is currently seeking a Senior Data Scientist to
join our rapidly growing team. The Data Lab is focused on applied research that
can be quickly brought to market as production services and new financial
services products.

This is an outstanding opportunity to be a part of a very small and talented
team, giving you the ability to help pioneer the usage of consumer
financial data science techniques, on data that is vastly more valuable and
complicated.

What you will do

As a Data Scientist, you will take lead on exploring and creating business
value from data science operations in new business data domains, working with
other data science and machine learning professionals, you will formulate
strategies for Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and follow-on data science
processes and architectures, including statistical analysis, machine learning
modeling etc. You will help ensure understanding of the business objectives and
help invent new ways to analyze, visualize, and derive value from enterprise
and financial services data.

Key Qualifications

  • PhD in Statistics or equivalent
  • 3+ years commercial machine learning experience
  • A strong understanding of machine learning theory.
  • Mid-level programming experience. Python, Java or C++ experience is highly
    desired.
  • A principled approach to solving algorithmic problems with a focus on what
    will make users happy.
  • A pragmatic approach to rapidly evaluating new algorithmic ideas.
  • A very high attention to detail and ability to thoroughly think through
    problems.
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills on both technical and
    non-technical topics.
  • Strong understanding of R or python ML packages and statistics.
  • Proven ability to mine a new data area and derive business value

Classification: Data Scientist, Finicity Data Innovation Lab
