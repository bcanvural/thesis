MT / NLP / Data Analytics Technologist

SDL Government is developing and deploying the next generation of machine
translation technology to enable our customer to access and process big data on
a global basis ? from social media exploitation to technical exploitation.
 Join SDL Government as the lead technologist and apply your real world
experience of data analysis, machine learning or Natural language processing to
our Machine Translation and Government Language Technology platform.

SDL Government?s machine translation and language translation technology
products are being integrated into Big Data Analytics tools across the US
government market. We seek a scientist who will lead the development of new and
creative concepts that integrate MT more effectively, across a diverse set of
network and cloud based environments, databases, data sets and data types.

The Opportunity:

  * Enjoy solving challenging issues and leading the development of concepts
    and proposals for cutting edge technology.
  * Align our MT solutions with other technology areas, integrating with state
    of the art approaches while dealing with real, heterogeneous big data.
  * Create concepts,  write proposals, and capture funding for government
    funded R&D ? then lead those R&D efforts.
  * Work with a dynamic team, in very fast-paced release cycles and contribute
    actively to the success of the company.

Responsibilities

  * Work on all aspects of the design, development and delivery of machine
    translation and Data analytics solutions.  This includes problem
    definition, data acquisition, data exploration and visualization, feature
    engineering, experimenting with MT algorithms, evaluating and comparing
    metrics, deploying the models and iteratively improving the solution
  * Build scalable, stable, and efficient processes to collect, manipulate,
    present, and analyze large datasets for production, leveraging experience
    in use-cases such as
      + Broadcast Web Monitoring
      + OCR
      + Speech to Text/Text to Speech
      + Cross Language Search/Chat
      + Linguistic Asset Management

  * Design, code, validate, automate, and scale software in a collaborative
    environment
  * Lead the company with a Passion for developing efficient, testable,
    scalable, and well-documented code

Requirements

  * 8+ years  with  MS degree or 2+ years with PhD
  * US Citizen
  * Security clearance, or ability to obtain security clearance
  * Familiarity with Artificial Intelligence and neural networks
  * Experience with data analytics projects involving machine learning/
    optimization in production environments.
  * Experience in mathematical optimization
