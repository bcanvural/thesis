VP of Data Operations and Quality

  * St. Louis, MO
  * Full Time
  * Senior Manager/Supervisor

  *  
  *  
  *  

About the Role:

We are looking for a process-driven, experienced data operations leader to
manage our data operations team. In this role you will help to scale our
current processes and teams to maximize data throughput and quality. Your
hands-on leadership style will get you into the weeds to identify and solve
problems quickly and drive scalable solutions. This position will report
directly to the CEO.

What you?ll be Responsible for:

  * Manage the Data Operations team comprised of internal Data Acquisition and
    Quality Assurance Analysts and offshore Business Process Outsourcers
    (BPOs), to accurately digitize product label data.
  * Maximize data throughput and quality utilizing BPOs, while also managing
    costs.
  * Manage established processes and identify and drive improvements and
    automation to ensure scalability.
  * Report regularly on data quality and throughput to internal teams,
    management, customers and Label Insight Board of Directors.
  * Learn and understand core technologies and axioms to drive process and
    automation improvements.
  * Manage a young and growing team of data analysts and challenge and inspire
    them to achieve success.

About You / Your Creds:

  * Education: Bachelor?s Degree Required--Bachelors of Science in Mathematics
    or Statistics is preferred.
  * 7-10+ years of experience; 5+ years experience in managing teams, with big
    data analysis experience a plus.
  * BPO experience
      + Data Processing
      + Similar to supply chain management
  * You are well-versed in the various aspects of setting up offshore teams
    (BPOs) and have demonstrated accomplishments in maximizing data throughput
    and data quality utilizing such teams, while also managing costs. 
  * Data Quality experience (not software quality)
      + You have previously managed data quality for a database.
      + You have proven experience in increasing data quality.
  * You are a hands-on leader, willing to get into the weeds to identify and
    solve problems, and communicate solutions to management.
  * You are a process-driven leader, who will drive scalable solutions.
  * You have both the knowledge and prior experience on reporting key data
    quality and throughput metrics.
  * Strong attention to detail and great communication skills are required.
  * You are a team player, who thrives in a fast-paced environment.
  * Prior BI experience is preferred.
  * Experience working in a technology focused environment is required.
  * Familiarity with SaaS environments and agile development and business
    methodologies is a plus.

Who we are:

Label Insight is high-growth technology company founded with the mission of
helping CPG companies and retailers help their consumers understand what?s in
the products they use and consume. Our data and tools focus on transforming
information about household products into simple, easy to use forms.? We?re
transforming the way brands and retailers interact with and share product data
in order to bring product transparency to the consumer.

Our team is unique because it?s composed not only of tech engineers and data
scientists, but food scientists, nutrition experts and product formulation
specialists, who enable the enriched attribution capability of our tools. We?ve
also attracted top senior executive talent with decades of leadership
experience to help manage our rapid growth and scale.

Our Values:

As a team, we share a common set of values: We are innovative problem solvers.
We believe in transparency, both in our product and in our work. We have
curious minds and aren?t afraid to challenge the status quo. We value
scientific inquiry. We aren?t afraid to take risks, and fail fast to learn fast
and improve fast. We embrace uniqueness. We are lifelong learners and we value
intellectual honesty.
