Title:
Job       University Recruiting - Non Tech

Location: US - California - San Francisco (HQ)

          criminal histories in a manner consistent with the requirements of

The Business Data Science at Salesforce.com is looking for a Data Science
Intern to develop tools and

technologies to help our business achieve extraordinary value with
Salesforce.com. We are looking for

someone to join our team to work on groundbreaking R&amp;D projects, to
leverage massive structured,

unstructured, transactional and real-time data sets from a variety of sources
and analyze customer usage

patterns and make actionable recommendations using statistics, data mining,
machine learning, business

understanding and common sense.

If you are a statistics, machine learning or data mining whiz (a.k.a. data
scientist) who is equally at home

discussing a project with business owners, customers, researchers or
developers, thrives on delivering

value to customers, is willing to roll his/her sleeves up and do the hard work,
and is extremely creative,

collaborative, innovative, yet disciplined, methodical and down to earth ? we
want to hear from you.

Work with us as we develop and apply state-of- the-art big-data techniques in
our cloud infrastructure.

The results of your work will help our customers be even more successful and
impact operations across

Salesforce.com.

In detail you will:

? Interact with internal clients to understand their requirements for
predictive analytics applications.

? Help develop and test predictive algorithms to be implemented as part of
various internal

Salesforce.com applications.

? Understand the business goals and initiatives and combine business modeling
skills with

outstanding data analysis.

? Cooperate with the Business Data Science team to design and execute
replicable data

acquisition and utilization processes. Acquiring, analyzing, cleaning and
structuring data is an

essential part of your daily responsibilities.

? Cooperate with IT and BI teams to ensure integration and implementation of
the algorithms you

have developed in the production environment.

In order to solve these challenges you should be able to leverage off-the-
shelf or open-source

technologies as well as in-house engineering, and feel comfortable with big
data solutions, applications

and infrastructure: 

Required Experience and Skills:

? Pursuing advanced degree (preferably Ph.D.) in stats, math, CS or other
relevant field.  Prior

work/publications in high quality cited scientific journals and conferences /
patents are a definite

plus

? Analytics experience including applied techniques in data mining, machine
learning, or graph

mining using R, Python, or Spark.

? Extensive experience with SQL

? Experience turning ideas into actionable designs. Able to persuade
stakeholders and champion

effective techniques through product development

? Comfortable working in a dynamic, research-oriented group with several
ongoing concurrent

projects

? Very strong verbal and written communication skills as well as excellent
presentation skills
