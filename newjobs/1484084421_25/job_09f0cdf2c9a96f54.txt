Procurement Analyst

Apply Company The Boeing Company Job ID 1600021622 Date posted 01/04/2017
Location Seattle Washington United States
Job Description

POSITION OVERVIEW:

 

This position supporting the Procurement Audit and People Processes group
within SM Operations. This position supports internal and external SM audits,
conducting special audits, development and execution of Corrective Action
Plans, and overall customer interface with DCMA/DCAA. This position also leads
the coordination and execution of Boot Camp training sessions and support BDS
Student Intern Program coordination and planning requirements. Other various
job duties will be project management of initiatives and process improvements,
and assisting in training and mentoring others. Knowledge of SM policies,
procedures and FAR are a must. Good communication both written and verbal, good
project management skills, good data analytical skills are required. Other soft
skills required: collaboration, initiative, decision making, communication,
working together. A good customer service mindset is necessary. A combination
of Audit/Self Governance, procurement, and related system knowledge preferred.

 

PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:

 

  • Leads development of processes and tools to measure key performance
    indicators.
  • Analyzes and prepares reports to measure key supplier and internal
    performance indicators.
  • Communicates strategic initiatives, projects or procurement activities.
    Leads cross-functional process management activities.
  • Leads self-assessment and audit teams to identify and implement corrective
    action.
  • Generates and analyzes data for compliance utilizing Boeing Supplier
    Management Procurement and Contracts systems/tools.
  • Leads self-governance efforts to mitigate risk and resolve issues of high
    risk programs.
  • Focal for internal/external audits.
  • Offers experience and mentorship to less experienced auditors in the
    organization.

Boeing is the world's largest aerospace company and leading manufacturer of
commercial airplanes and defense, space and security systems. We are engineers
and technicians. Skilled scientists and thinkers. Bold innovators and dreamers.
Join us, and you can build something better for yourself, for our customers and
for the world.
