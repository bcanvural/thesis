Job Description:
Seeking a Data Scientist to support  the Intelligence Community and at Special
Operations Command.

Job Duties Include (but not limited to):
•Solve complex data problems, discovering fresh insights, and delivering data
to the user that is timely and establishes unparalleled trust and confidence in
the data and services of our customers.
•Work closely with analytical cells to understand analyst behavior and their
use of data, systems, and tools.
•Provide interpretation of new data sources relevant to our user community and
recommend ways to apply the data to identify unique insights.
•Identify and analyze data shortfalls, hidden insights or anomalies and make
recommendations on ways to apply the data and help to codify new data
collection or system requirements.
•Identify discrepancies in the tactics, techniques or procedures (TTPs)
currently used by analysts in the course of their varied mission sets and
provide recommendations to improve (TTPs), methodologies, and tools and create
efficiencies.
•Identify hidden insights or anomalies contained within the data and work with
analysts and engineers to solve complex data problems facing a wide spectrum of
users.
•Analyze and interpret the results of software solutions which are developed,
measure and test the analytic results and communicate outcomes to
decision-makers.
•Identify, understand, and decompose analytical problems and hypothesize
approaches for answering those questions and assist in the development of
capabilities and techniques to extract actionable insights and derive
meaningful information from a variety of complex data sources containing
structured, unstructured and sensor data. 
•Attend forums and conferences and engage with industry and academia to ensure
cognizance of the latest trends, technologies, and capabilities in the big data
domain.
•Provide recommendations to current users on systems training methodologies to
ensure that the current user community is employing tools and capabilities
appropriately.
•Work with analysts to develop and document improvements or changes in the
tactics, techniques or procedures if and when new approaches to the data
require changes to TTPs or workflows. 

Desired Skills:
•Demonstrated interest and experience in empirical research.
•Experience with identifying relationships, patterns and factors that drive
data sets using statistical methods such as regression, correlations,
clustering, and principle components.
•Ability to examine how data from multiple disparate sources creates
relationships, patterns, associations and other factors that drive data sets
and visualizations.
•Experience with defining new data collection methods as well as new system
requirements for processing and visualizing data.
•Experience working with data generated from smart devices, web, mobile and
social media and build “mash ups” to uncover new insights. 
•Experience with developing predictive models, algorithms and best practices
for answering analytical questions.
•Ability to engineer systems that organize and funnel data through the most
efficient available configuration possible and translate the most valuable data
into meaningful discussions that assist analysts and decision makers in problem
solving.
•Ability to merge data sources together, ensure consistency of data-sets, frame
visualization techniques to aid in understanding data, build mathematical
models using the data, present and communicate the data insights/findings to
various team members and leadership. 
•Experience with preparing project reports detailing actionable insights,
recommendations and courses of actions to operationalize those recommendations
as well as the tasks necessary to operationalize those recommendations. 

Required Skills & Experience:
•10+ years of related experience in data analysis is required
•Hands-on experience in the following programming / scripting languages:
Python, php, MATLAB, SAS, Numpy/Scipy
•Experience working with noisy intelligence and operational data sets from
multiple disparate sources to include unstructured, structured and sensor data
in many disciplines such as all-source reporting, geographic information
systems, biometrics reporting, imagery intelligence, signals intelligence,
human intelligence, and open source intelligence.
•Experience working with large data sets and with distributed computing
technologies such as MapReduce/Hadoop and Hive.
•Ability to communicate data analysis results in a clear and concise manner to
a variety of technical and non-technical audiences.
•Ability to identify and understand an intelligence analytical cell’s questions
and translate responses into technical solutions and recommend innovative
technology based solutions to meet those requirements.
•Must be able to define and document appropriate advanced analytic methods,
predictive analysis, pattern analysis, and the like.
•Define and document appropriate advanced analytic methods for selection,
categorization, and predictive modeling.
•Create algorithms or predictive models that can be put into production and
will transition the algorithms and models to the engineering team.
•Actively monitor those algorithms and provide recommendations to update as
needed. 
•Develop and document methodologies for analyzing all types of data and
determine patterns in the data.  Data may include social media, open source,
signals intelligence, human intelligence as prioritized by the user community.
• MUST BE A US CITIZEN
