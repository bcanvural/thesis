Description

Palo Alto Networks® is the fastest-growing security company in history.  We
offer the chance to be part of an important mission: ending breaches and
protecting our way of digital life. If you are a motivated, intelligent,
creative, and hardworking individual, then this job is for you!

The IT organization at Palo Alto Networks is undergoing major transformation to
help the company scale rapidly and a key strategic enabler is our ability to
become a predictive enterprise. With that in mind, we are looking to hire the
leader of the Big Data and Analytics organization in IT. This leader will help
lead and scale out the Big Data and Analytics team with a focus on maturing
practices around Data Science, Big Data and database technologies, data
quality, data governance, and business intelligence.

In this role, your work and leadership will broadly influence the company’s
ability to execute on its strategy and decision making. This position will
report directly to the CIO at Palo Alto Networks

Responsibilities:

  • Lead and develop a world class team of 20+ data scientists, engineers,
    product managers, and analysts
  • Collaborate with various business organizations including product
    management, sales, marketing, customer success, and service delivery teams
    on advanced big data technology to support revenue growth
  • Provide thought leadership around Big data and data science especially in
    areas of building scalable data lake, predictive modelling techniques, and
    unlocking Palo Alto Networks unique data set.
  • Define and deliver on a multi-year strategic roadmap on Big Data and
    Analytics at Palo Alto Networks IT
  • Evangelize the creative use of data and analytics to solve business
    problems
  • Ability to bring data to life through visualization and story telling
  • Implement an Agile development process for on-going projects or ad-hoc
    requests
  •  

Qualifications:

  • Graduate level degree required (Masters preferred) with background in
    Statistics, Economics, Mathematics, Engineering, or similar quantitative
    discipline
  • 15+ years of overall software engineering experience with 10+ years of
    experience with advanced statistical techniques, including concepts of
    machine-learning, predictive modeling, and business intelligence.
  • 5+ years of experience managing and building Big Data environments
    including experience in building and scaling out Data lake environments on
    premise or in the cloud
  • Proven experience leading and inspiring a high-performing team to support
    growth objectives
