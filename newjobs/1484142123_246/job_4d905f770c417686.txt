Career Opportunity

Quantitative Analyst II

Location Bridgeport, Connecticut Job ID/ Req. Number167169 Category Risk
Management Full/Part Time F Regular/Temporary 1st Shift

Description

The Senior Quantitative Analyst in Credit Card Portfolio Management and
Acquisitions is a key member of the team that leads the portfolio risk
management activities for Citizens Credit Card portfolio.
The responsibilities for this position include: research and development of
quantitative strategies in authorizations, credit line optimization, and
account reissues and closure; track, identify, and evaluate opportunities to
improve card portfolio, including design of champion/challenger test;
collaborate with colleagues around the organization, as well as external
vendors; support programs initiated by business partners across the company;
perform statistical analysis for various structured and ad hoc projects to
support other risk managers in order to achieve overall risk management
strategic objectives.
The successful candidate should have a strong quantitative background, product
analytics experience, a deep understanding of basic statistical concepts
(hypothesis testing, confidence intervals, design of experiments, sample size
determination, statistical significance etc.), strong excel modeling experience
and familiarity addressing business problems through fact based and scientific
analytics preferred.
Specific responsibilities include, but not limited to:

  * Perform deep dive analytics including Cohort analysis, segmentation
    exercises, credit loss projections and ad-hoc analysis that would provide
    insights to the business.
  * Develop and execute credit risk strategies that support pricing, credit
    line optimization, credit card reissue, payment fraud prevention strategies
    and inactive credit card closure strategies.  
  * Must have excellent analytical skills, including ability to query
    databases, sort through complex data to crystallize business implications,
    and drive value for the business 
  * Lead/participate in analytical projects. Facilitate meetings, assign/
    produce action items; work with business partners across the organization
    and with external vendors, with special attention to deadlines and
    interdependent projects across the enterprise.
  * Make observations and implement recommendations that contribute to a strong
    regulatory (internal and external) environment.
  * Reporting on monthly Key Performance Indicator(KPI) metrics for the
    portfolio

Qualifications

  * At least 2 years of risk analytical experience; preferably in consumer
    finance or at least 5 years of experience in a related quantitative field
    with a proven track record of addressing business problems through fact
    based and scientific analytics
  * SAS programming skills and SQL experience.
  * Experience working in fast-paced and agile environments
  * Demonstrated critical thinking and problem solving skills, comfortable
    working with ambiguity, adapt easily to changes and to fast pace
    environment.
  * Exceptional communication skills, both written and verbal

Education:

  * Bachelors Degree or Advanced degree (M.S. or Ph.D.) in
    Mathematics, Statistics, Econometrics, Operations Research, Engineering, or
    related quantitative field

Hours & Work Schedule

Hours per Week:  40

Work Schedule:  M-F 8:00-5:00
