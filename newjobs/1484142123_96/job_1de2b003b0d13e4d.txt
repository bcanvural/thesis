As an Analytics professional, you will be part of a team that has
enterprise-wide responsibility for establishing consistent and predictable
process execution, measuring performance and process health, optimizing
business decision-making through the use of advanced analytical models, and
focusing our resources on the most critical issues and driving
cross-organizational solutions. Will include interaction with data scientists
who develop supporting algorithms.

Specific analytic responsibilities for this position will include:
- Solve complex challenges involving data modeling, scenario creation, risk
analysis, data mining/validation, and development of business rules to guide
technology development.
- Ability to document requirements, designs and detailed technical
specifications, training materials, operating instructions, and test plans to
support development of predictive analytic solutions.
- Partner with business & technical experts to identify analytic opportunities
and define requirements.
- Data modeling skills and competency
- Demonstrated knowledge of linkage between data and process
- Incorporate technology capabilities into design development.
