Data Scientist
Apply Now    
JobDescription :

 

Position Description

The Role: 
S&P Global Market Intelligence, a premier sector-focused news,and financial
information provider is looking for a Data Scientist to join our Technology
team in our Charlottesville, VA office.   


The Impact: 
As a Data Scientist, you will be responsible for the execution of data science
projects that deliver novel, data-driven insights, and innovative data products
that solve high-impact business problems for both S&P GMI’s customers and
internal clients.


The Career Opportunity: 
S&P Global Market Intelligence is a recognized industry-leading provider of
data and analytics; as such, it provides an unusually rich environment for a
data scientist to make an impact and grow personally and professionally. In
addition to applying data science, you will have an opportunity to help
establish best practices for a growing data-science team.


The Team / The Business: 
The Data Science team is tasked with applying data science techniques to solve
high-impact business problems for both S&P Global Market Intelligence’s
customers and internal clients. This includes working with business
stakeholders to identify opportunities, implement solutions according to data
science best practices, and deploy solutions into production software systems.


Job Responsibilities:

  • Design, execute and deploy data analysis and modeling projects aimed at
    solving high-impact business problems for both S&P GMI’s customers and
    internal clients

  • Play a central role in all stages of the data-science project life cycle,
    including:

  • Identification of suitable data-science project opportunities

  • Partnering with business leaders, domain experts, and end-users to gain
    business understanding, data understanding, and collect requirements

  • Data preparation

  • Data analysis/modeling

  • Evaluation/interpretation of results and presentation to business leaders

  • Partnering with software developers to provide specifications for deploying
    models/algorithms into production systems, when applicable

  • Prepare the exploratory data analyses, proof-of-concept modeling, and
    business cases necessary to generate stakeholder buy-in and internal
    support for your data-science projects

  • Proactively communicate the vision and status of your data-science
    projects, ensuring that accurate expectations are set and met across all
    levels of stakeholders


Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Math, Statistics, Engineering, Computer Science, or
    related field; or equivalent experience

  • 1-3+ years’ experience in a data-science or advanced data-analysis role

  • Demonstrated success in leveraging large amounts of data to solve
    analytical problems

  • Strong English speaking and writing skills required

  • Occasional travel is required. 


Experience and Proficiencies:

  • Familiarity with data analysis scripting languages, such as Python or R
    (Python preferred)

  • Knowledge of advanced statistical data analysis and modeling

  • Experience with data mining, machine learning, natural language processing,
    and data visualization

  • Working knowledge of traditional data storage technologies, e.g. relational
    databases, SQL, and ETL tools

  • Exposure to “big data” technologies, e.g. Hadoop, Spark, NoSQL data stores

  • Familiarity with conventional software development languages (Scala,
    JavaScript, C#, Java, etc.) a plus

  • Ability to collaborate effectively with a diverse group of business,
    operational and technical stakeholders

  • Ability to communicate complex mathematical models and processes in
    straightforward, non-technical language
