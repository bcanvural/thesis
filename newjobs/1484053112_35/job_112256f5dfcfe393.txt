AgilOne

Data Scientist

  • Sunnyvale, CA
  • Full-time

Company Description

AgilOne, the Customer Data and Engagement Hub, provides enterprise consumer
marketers the power to integrate customer data across digital, physical, and
mobile channels, deliver customer analytics with predictive insights and
360-degree profiles, and engage customers at every touch point in order to
maximize lifetime value. Currently, the AgilOne solution supports more than 150
brands worldwide

We leverage the latest technologies in big data, machine learning and data
quality management to deliver an enterprise-grade, scalable and high
performance tool for marketers such as Tumi, Lilly Pulitzer and David’s Tea.

AgilOne is backed by Sequoia Capital, Tenaya, and Mayfield. We are looking for
the very best talent to join the AgilOne family.

Job Description

As a Professional Services Data Scientist, you are in charge of designing,
building and deploying complex technical custom projects in particular around
data integration, data processing and data analytics. You will also work
closely with clients, account managers and product implementation to understand
client’s business use cases, define business and technical requirements and
communicate deliverables.

In order to succeed in this position, you should be autonomous, analytical, a
self-starter and organized. You are adaptable and thrive on working with
minimal structure and guidance. You are also excited about working with
customers -- both internally and externally.

As an experienced professional, you are expected to manage and drive forward
multiple complex projects and act as a mentor to less experienced team members

We work hard to maintain our fast-paced, friendly culture and help us maintain
our leadership position in the retail industry and beyond.


Responsibilities

  • Develop a deep expertise of AgilOne’s platform architecture, data layers
    and capabilities
  • Work with clients, account managers and product to understand the client’s
    business use cases and data
  • Design, build, and deploy elegant custom solutions and extensions to the
    platform in particular around data structures, data integrations, data
    analytics and data models
  • Write production-quality code and technical documentation
  • Troubleshoot product and data anomalies and ensure absolute integrity of
    deliverables
  • Use project, delivery and reporting tools including Salesforce and
    Mavenlink
  • Work with internal product and engineering teams and contribute to the
    continued enhancement and improvement of our platform

Qualifications

• Bachelor&apos;s Degree in Engineering, Information Technology, Computer
Science, Mathematics or similar technical/analytical degree
• Strong academic qualifications, including advanced understanding/coursework
in database management and math (Linear, Algebra, Calculus)\
• 2+ years of experience in a data analytics position
• Solid programming skills in SQL, TSql, PL/SQL or similar database programming
language
• Proficiency in statistical data analysis and data mining packages (e.g R,
Python)
• Intellectual curiosity, along with excellent problem-solving and quantitative
skills, including the ability to disaggregate issues, identify root causes and
recommend solutions
• Self-motivated and good sense of ownership - comfortable working in a fast
paced environment with limited direction
• Excellent organizational skills and ability to manage multiple tasks
simultaneously
• Excellent written and verbal communication skills tailored to an audience of
professionals, management and staff with a broad range of technical skills
• Humility and sense of humor

Nice to Have:
• Experience with ETL tools (e.g SQL Server Integration Services)
• Understanding and experience of integrating with APIs. Knowledge of standards
such as REST, JSON, XML, SOAP and OAuth
• Strong object oriented programming skills (e.g Java, JavaScript, Scala)
• Understanding of the Cloudera Big Data Stack. Hands on experience with Hive,
Impala and Spark.
• Experience with digital analytics concepts (e.g Next Product to buy, cross
channel attribution) and tools digital analytic (e.g. Google Analytics,
CoreMetrics, SiteCatalyst)
• Prior IT consulting and/or project management experience
• Master’s Degree or MBA with a technical undergraduate degree
