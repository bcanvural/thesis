Date: 04-Jan-2017

Location: Hoffman Estates, IL, US

Company: Tate & Lyle

                                        

                                        

Senior Research Engineer ? Provides engineering expertise to successfully
develop, pilot, and commercialize new processes and products. Contributes to
group compliance of all relevant Safety, OSHA, EPA, and Corporate guidelines.
Effectively communicates project goals and progress to key stakeholders.
Participates in defining resource requirements to achieve assigned
responsibilities including Process Technology personnel and Pilot equipment.
Designs lab, pilot and commercial trials and then evaluates and documents test
results. Coordinates Engineering Technicians and Lab Technicians to produce
relevant and reliable data during Pilot Plant trials. Applies sound engineering
principals to innovation of process development and reduction of capital and
operating costs. Develops and confirms economic evaluations for new products
and processes utilizing existing research formats and databases. Prepares mass
and energy balances for new and existing processes. Designs and directs
installation of new pilot processes. Supports manufacturing through data
evaluation, new technology implementation, and troubleshooting. Participates in
startups and launches of new processes at commercial scale with global
opportunity. Works directly with chemists, biologists, engineers, associate
scientists, operations personnel and engineering technicians to develop novel
processes at lab, pilot, and commercial scale. Develops and provides expertise
in unit operation performance and integration to optimize economic return on
new products and processes. Provides guidance to science teams on critical lab
scale development criteria. Capable of working independently to evaluate,
select, and apply broad engineering/scientific techniques. Mentors junior
staff.

 

Requirements ?Master's or foreign equivalent degree  in Chemical Engineering or
related engineering field and five years of progressive experience in the job
offered or five years of progressive  industrial experience in new product
development from concept to commercialization or technical support of
manufacturing operations. Special skills - Experience with corn wet milling
technology including plant operational exposure; food ingredient operations
including downstream  purification and refining unit operations; integrating
mass and energy balance models with cash cost of manufacture estimating;
modeling capital cost estimating of new processes and process expansions; new
product and process development including scale-up at Pilot Plant level;
transferring new technology to plant scale execution including direct
participation in commissioning and startup; solvent (non-aqueous) processing
and solvent recovery; and SAP business management systems.  Occasional domestic
and international travel required.   

 

Work location: Tate & Lyle Ingredients Americas LLC, 5450 Prairie Stone
Parkway, Hoffman Estates, IL 60192

Apply online at: http://www.tateandlyle.com/careers/Pages/Careers.aspx

 


Nearest Major Market: Chicago
