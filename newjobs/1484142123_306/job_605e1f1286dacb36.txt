Overview

Germany SAS

 

The Machine Learning algorithms team at SAS has a graduate summer
internship available for highly qualified, motivated, and innovative students.

The SAS Intern Program will allow you to partner with leadership to work on
business-critical projects resulting in an immediate impact to SAS and our
customers. You could be contributing code to our industry-leading software or
ensuring the quality of our products for our Fortune 100 customers ? the sky is
the limit!

 

The graduate internship is open to doctoral and master candidates in applied
mathematics, computer science, electrical and computer engineering, operations
research, statistics and related graduate departments, the internship offers
the opportunity to assist in the research and development of new machine
learning algorithms in the high performance world. You will contribute to
activities such as literature review, research, development, publish at top
level research conferences, and prepare demos for business oriented ML
conferences. You will work closely with professional machine learning experts
who develop software used in many areas throughout the world.

 

Essential

  * Ph.D student in applied mathematics, computer science, electrical and
    computer engineering, operations research, statistics, and related graduate
    departments
  * Coursework in machine learning, data mining, statistical modeling.
  * Demonstrated experience in machine learning algorithms beyond the
    coursework.
  * Demonstrated programming experience in languages like C, C++, Python

Preferences

  * Research experience in computational aspects of machine learning
    algorithms.
  * Research and development experience in active learning and online learning
    algorithms.
  * Knowledge in exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, predictive
    modelling and visualization techniques.
  * Experience in distributed/parallel algorithms.
