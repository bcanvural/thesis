Applied Research Scientist, Machine Learning / Deep Learning

Samsung Research America (Collective Rationality Team)

Mountain View, CA

 

Position Summary:

 

Samsung Research America?s Computer Science Innovation Center is currently
seeking a talented, competent, and highly motivated individual to join our
Collective Rationality team as an Applied Research Scientist. The Collective
Rationality team is an interdisciplinary and hands-on research team focused on
eliminating unnecessary trial and error of individuals? experience by offering
innovatively-analyzed results from existing experiences, comparisons,
evaluations and decisions.

 

The successful candidate will research and develop practical ML/AI algorithms
for applying them to solve exciting problems with massive data and will work
with other team members to build software prototypes and create
proof-of-concept technology demonstrations.

 

Responsibilities:

 

-   Develop scalable algorithms based on state-of-the-art machine learning and
neural network methodologies

-   Contribute to the team?s research and development activities; participate
and lead some of the efforts.

-   Formulate research problems based on real-world needs and conduct
high-quality research.

-   Apply expert software development skills to a wide range of ML-related
coding projects

-   Develop prototypes and execute experiments to evaluate the performance of
ML/DL architectures/algorithms in large-scale industrial applications

-   Write well-structured and re-usable code in programming languages such as
Python, C/C++, etc.

-   Create proof-of-concept technology demonstrations.

 

Qualifications:

 

-   MS/Ph.D. in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related field

-   Broad and deep knowledge of machine learning and/or deep learning research

-   Experience building systems based on machine learning and/or deep learning
methods

-   Programming experience in Python, C or C++

-   Achievements in competitions such as Kaggle, ILSVRC or WMT a strong plus

-   Experience solving analytical problems using quantitative approaches a plus
