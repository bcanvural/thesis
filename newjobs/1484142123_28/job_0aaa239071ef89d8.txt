Date: Jan 11, 2017

Location: San Jose, CA, US

Company: Cisco Systems, Inc.

Additional Location(s) or Information:

Job Category: Engineer - Software

Level of Experience: Experienced - Non Manager

Requisition #: R1015134

 

Description:

What You'll Do
Analyze large data sets to develop new models and algorithms to predict
application performance, trend, and cost etc.

Develop data driven algorithms for optimization application performance while
minimize cost.

Improve accuracy of models through automated backtesting frameworks.

Provide analytics support for reporting and data acquisition strategies.

Improve processes in Data Acquisition, Data Modeling, Knowledge Representation
and Inference across a variety of structured and unstructured data and data
sources including relational, graph, and columnar databases

Who You'll Work With
Senior Data Scientist Cloud Application Analytics

Our technology changes the way the world works, lives, plays and learns. We
connect everything - people, process, data and things. The Internet of
Everything is driving rapid innovations in cloud analytics. Cloud native
applications, full stack monitoring and predictive analytics are paving the way
for a self-healing infrastructure.

We are helping customers discover, predict and optimize their infrastructure
through intelligence derived from telemetry data. This SaaS based service is
designed to process billions of events spanning clouds, applications, and
infrastructure and converting that into actionable insights in real time. The
team consists of Data Scientists, Software Architects & Developers, Product
Managers and Designers. Are you entrepreneur who wants to make impact inside a
company that can scale your product? You?ll lead a ?start-up? software
engineering team that is passionate about transforming the industry.

We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join our Data Science Team. The
person will work alongside industry-leading cloud computing technologists and
data scientists to solve large-scale, complex, and challenging problems in
performing both predictive analysis and prescriptive analysis for applications
in the cloud and their underlying infrastructure.

Who You Are
Masters Degree or PhD in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics or similar
field of study, and more than 3 years of equivalent work experience in machine
learning.

Experience in developing data-driven algorithms in performance modeling, system
optimization,and machine learning.

Experience working with distributed systems, and strong understanding of
technologies like Spark, Hadoop/Hive, mySQL etc.

Experience with R, Scikit-learn, H20, data mining, machine learning,
statistical modeling tools and underlying algorithms.

Programming experience in one or more of the languages like Python/Shell/C/Java
/Scala.

Attitude to thrive in a fun, fast-paced start-up environment with stellar team
of fellow data analysts and programmers.

Why Cisco
We connect everything: people, processes, data, and things. We innovate
everywhere, taking bold risks to shape the technologies that give us smart
cities, connected cars, and handheld hospitals. And we do it in style with
unique personalities who aren?t afraid to change the way the world works,
lives, plays and learns.

We are thought leaders, tech geeks, pop culture aficionados, and we even have a
few purple haired rock stars. We celebrate the creativity and diversity that
fuels our innovation. We are dreamers and we are doers.

We Are Cisco.
