Overview:

ORC International helps the world’s leaders to optimize their businesses today,
understand how they can win tomorrow, and realize the future. A global top 20
business intelligence firm with a stellar track record in quality, leading edge
analytical know-how and data integration supported by an agile technology
platform, ORC International enables leading organizations to better understand
their people and transform their overall business performance. Proud of our
long-standing heritage, deep client connections and ongoing partnership with
CNN, we continually grow and evolve as our relationships do- providing
relevant, connected and evidence-based insight and perspective to answering
today’s business challenges.

 

ORC’s work environment is supportive, enriching and fun. We offer competitive
salaries and rich benefits including health, dental, vision, 401(k) with
company match, PTO, Paid Holidays, Community Volunteer days, seasonal outings
and more.  ORC International is an Equal Opportunity Employer: M/F/D/V.

Responsibilities:

  • Manage and perform advanced data analysis and insightful reporting across
    projects of varying complexity
  • Build data visulaization capabilities in advanced analytics team  
  • Research and stay up to date with current data visualization trends
  • Demonstrate excellent creative reporting capabilities
  • Demonstrate passion for data, analyzing it, visualizing it either static or
    interactive and telling the story for our clients
  • Solid understanding of common analytical research methods,  advanced
    analytical methods are a plus
  • Participate in client presentations
  • Exhibit great communication skills and comfort in client
    facing opportunities relative to the analysis
  • Provide design and methodology support at pre-sale phase
  • Mentor and participate in the development of junior and mid-level
    colleagues to ensure the long term vibrancy and scalability of the Advanced
    Analytics team
  • Ownership of analytical phase of projects

 

 

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree or MBA in Marketing or related field
  • SPSS statistical software analysis experience required
  • Experience with Adobe Creative Cloud (PhotoShop, Illustrator), D3 or
    similar data visualization software required
  • R, Python, and Tableau or other advanced data integration and programming
    software required
  • Excellent client facing/presentation skills relative to the analysis
  • Five plus years analytical experience on the market research supplier or
    client side
  • Demonstrate excellent creative reporting capabilities
  • Demonstrate solid understanding of common analytical research methods
  • Eagerness to learn and conduct more advanced analytical methods
