Date: Jan 4, 2017

Location: Palo Alto, CA, US

Company: SAP

Requisition ID: 132416
Work Area: Software-Design and Development
Expected Travel: 0 - 10%
Career Status: Professional
Employment Type: Regular Full Time

 

COMPANY DESCRIPTION

 

As market leader in enterprise application software, SAP helps companies of all
sizes and industries innovate through simplification. From the back office to
the boardroom, warehouse to storefront, on premise to cloud, desktop to mobile
device ? SAP empowers people and organizations to work together more
efficiently and use business insight more effectively to stay ahead of the
competition. SAP applications and services enable customers to operate
profitably, adapt continuously, and grow sustainably.

 

We, at the Innovation Center Silicon Valley, create prototypes, technology
frameworks and new business solutions with strategic customers, start-ups and
leading research institutions. We?re looking for a passionate data scientist to
lead the identification and exploration of whole new focus areas and to
engineer Enterprise AI solutions based on the most recent research results in
Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing.
 

Background

We are part of SAP?s Innovation Center Network which is headed by SAP?s Chief
Innovation Officer Juergen Mueller. We are an interdisciplinary team from
around the world with skills and experiences ranging from architecture,
consulting, data science, visual & interaction design, research, to software
sales and engineering and we are looking for a passionate leader & builder with
a proven track record in applied machine learning.
 

Purpose

If you are an experienced data scientist who is looking for an exciting
opportunity to explore whole new areas, to apply machine learning to complex
business challenges and specifically to enable SAP?s users to interact with
systems using natural language in a variety of ways. This role combines (1)
experience with machine learning, (2) practical knowledge of working with
scalable platforms for processing of huge data sets, (3) the ability to teach
machines to understand natural language in robust ways, (4) building and
maintaining innovative new products from the ground-up, and scaling from
minimum viable product up to shippable production code. Be part of a team that
rewards taking risks and drives new product ideas and development in the.
 

Responsibilities
* Guiding development teams applying cutting-edge research insights in Machine
Learning and Natural Language Processing to challenges of SAP?s customers.
* Contributing to the efficiency, effectiveness, and improvement of ICSV data
science best practices as a thought leader, as an advisor to the leadership of
the Innovation Center Silicon Valley as well as SAP?s Chief Innovation Officer.
* Influence & multiply knowledge and skills across SAP?s product development
organization with over 20,000 employees.

Requirements
* At least 5 years of experience in deep learning / machine learning / data
science development projects for innovative products.
* M.S. or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Machine Learning, Mathematics, Statistics,
Operations Research or related field
* Deep knowledge of ML/DL frameworks and NLP technologies
* Statistics, predictive modeling, operations research, informatics and tools,
e.g., SPSS, SAS, R, Python
* Track record of developing novel learning algorithms and/or systems
* Software development experience with Java, C++, JSON, Python, XML
* Cloud-based experience with PaaS and IaaS, as well as Cloud-based, agile,
development environments.
* Strong enthusiasm and interest for new technologies and business models of
the next generation
* Ability to visualize data and present core insights in a clear and compelling
way
* Excellent written and communication skills in English language
* Experience working in a fast-paced environment with a lean product
development process through rapid iteration
 

Location & Travel
* Palo Alto, ~10% travel
