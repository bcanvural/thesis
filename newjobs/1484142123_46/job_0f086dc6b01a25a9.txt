Description

Phreesia is looking for a Data Scientist to join our growing analytics and data
science team. This individual will provide technical and analytical expertise
and support to Associate Director of Data Science and will work
cross-functionally to define and create analytic tools and approaches to solve
business problems. They will work to integrate and identify insights from
various large data sets to produce high quality, timely analyses to help make
informed business decisions.

This role requires the ability to harness the power of data science and big
data technologies to continually improve our ability to make smart decision and
optimize business practices internally, as well discover valuable insights for
our clients. You will join a team of exceptionally smart, humorous,
quick-witted, and dedicated coworkers, all passionate about data and
healthcare. Above all else, you should love solving complex problems, and be
committed to constant improvement, both of yourself and those around you.


Responsibilities:

  * Drawing insights from various diverse data sources to tell a story about
    Phreesia?s value

  * Creating visually-intriguing presentations to convey findings to team
    member and clients.
  * Define and implement methodology improvements for existing processes.
  * Lead development of new methodologies and approaches.
  * Manage ad-hoc analysis and reporting requests.

Qualifications:

  * Bachelor?s degree required
  * Highly proficient in SQL
  * 2+ years of experience with a data analysis tools (python, pandas, numpy,
    R, etc.)
  * Experience with one or more data visualization tools (D3, Tableau, plotly,
    etc.)
  * Excellent written and oral communications skills with the ability to
    seamlessly navigate technical, analytical, and business audiences

Preferred:

  * Healthcare industry experience
  * Familiarity or experience with machine learning algorithms a plus
  * Graduate degree in Mathematics/Statistics or related field a plus



Phreesia is proud to be an equal opportunity employer.

Phreesia would like to thank all applicants for submitting their resume,
however, only applicants selected to be interviewed will be contacted.
