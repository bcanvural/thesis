2017 Research Scientist

Job Description

Come join the Intel Labs Security and Privacy Research Group. We are looking
for a systems and security expert to conduct research on next generation data
center security problems. This includes the security of personal and enterprise
data in emerging cloud computing paradigms, including technologies such as
OpenStack, as well as the security of data that is shared between the cloud and
mobile devices such as phones and tablets. You will identify new security
trends, and develop prototypes to evaluate and improve the latest platform
security technologies. You will have the opportunity to showcase your work at
internal and external forums and your innovations will lay the foundation for
future Intel products.


Qualifications

Qualifications:
You must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered
for this position. Preferred qualifications are in addition to the minimum
requirements and are considered a plus factor in identifying top candidates.
Experience listed below would be obtained through relevant previous job/
internship and/or school/research experiences.
Minimum Qualifications:
The candidate must have a MS degree or a PhD degree in Computer Science,
Computer Engineering, or other science/engineering related field.
- Minimum 1 year of Strong systems programming and debug experience in Linux
- Minimum 1 year experience with Virtualization and VMM internals/development
- Minimum 1 year Knowledge of x86 processor concepts like paging features,
protected mode, etc.
- Minimum 1 year experience/understanding of fundamental security concepts
Desired Qualifications:
Linux OS internals, Security vulnerabilities & attacks, TXT, SGX, Crypto API, a
plus.

Inside this Business Group

Intel Labs is the company's world-class, industry leading research
organization, responsible for driving Intel's technology pipeline and creating
new opportunities. The mission of Intel Labs is to deliver breakthrough
technologies to fuel Intel's growth. This includes identifying and exploring
compelling new technologies and high risk opportunities ahead of business unit
investment and demonstrating first-to-market technologies and innovative new
usages for computing technology. Intel Labs engages the leading thinkers in
academia and industry in addition to partnering closely with Intel business
units.
