Date: Jan 3, 2017

Location: LAKE FOREST, IL, US, 60045-5202

Company: Grainger

Company Overview 

Grainger is a global leading broad line supplier of facilities maintenance
products serving businesses and institutions. Our 18,000 employees are driven
to serve customers and the community in exceptional ways focusing on delivering
the highest level of service. The Grainger team works closely with customers to
better understand their challenges and provide cost-saving solutions.
Grainger's employees serve customers more than 115,000 times every day through
multiple channels. As part of a high-performing team, you?ll be able to develop
your talents, and make a difference. Grainger is a Fortune 500company and a
perennial member of Fortune magazine's Most Admired Companies list.

 

 

Position Description 

Data Science application areas span across Recommender Systems, Semantic Search
Applications, Resource Optimization Solutions, etc. Projects often encompass
research, design, coding, testing, scaling and implementation.

 

Senior / Lead Data Scientists help develop dynamic solutions using statistical,
machine learning and deep learning methodologies. They are responsible for
development of one of more solution components and are expected to lead one of
the work streams in a project with minimal supervision.

 

  * Transform data into actionable insights and recommendations. Present clear
    and concise results (70%)
      + Take on large, complex tasks and execute with minimal guidance
      + Data mining using state-of-the-art methods.
      + Develop and validate algorithms and models.
      + Processing, cleansing, and verifying the integrity of data used for
        analysis
  * Demonstrate thought leadership and contribute to solution development
    process (20%)

Provide technical guidance to junior team members (10%)

 

 

 

Position Requirements: 

PhD or equivalent preferred.

3-5 years of experience in related field preferred.

  * Experience in usage of Statistical and Machine Learning algorithms -
    Regression, Decision Trees, Ensemble, Bayesian, Clustering etc. (any 3).
  * Proficient with R, Python, Scala, SQL, Spark etc. (any 3 of these)
  * Familiarity with Neural Networks / Deep Learning / Natural Language
    Processing (NLP). Proficiency preferred.
  * Experience in NLP applications is a plus
  * PhD in relevant area with 2+ years? experience
  * Masters in Data Science / Advanced Analytics with 4+ years relevant
    experience (or)
  * Optional (some familiarity in one or two of these areas may be relevant
    needed depending on the project we are hiring for at the time)
      + Query languages such as Hive, Pig, SQL, Spark
      + REST API?s to leverage disparate data sources

Scripting / programming skills: Java, C++ etc.
