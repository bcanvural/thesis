Job Description

Summary/Objective:
This position requires an analyst to synthesize and leverage various datasets
to enhance sales, marketing,
and overall corporate objectives. This position is part of a multidisciplinary
team and work on high-impact
projects utilizing data analytics and machine learning.
This position will be part of the team that combines computer science,
mathematics, and substantive
expertise to identify and inject high-value insights at their point of highest
impact. The team works in quick
iterations, using the techniques and algorithms best suited for solving
challenging problems.
Essential Job Functions:
 Advocate, evangelize and build data-driven products that help our customers
achieve their goals.
 Become an expert on current and future datasets, extending data with third
party sources of
information when needed.
 Produce new and creative analytic solutions and present results in a clear
manner.
 Work with cross-functional team members to identify and prioritize
actionable, high-impact insights
across a variety of core business areas.
 Research, design, implement and validate cutting-edge algorithms to analyze
diverse sources of
data to achieve targeted outcomes.
 Provide expertise on mathematical concepts for the broader applied analytics
team and inspire the
adoption of advanced analytics and data science across the organization.
Core Competencies:
 Communicating effectively
 Building relationships & teamwork
 Customer/ consumer focus
 Initiative and results orientation
 Planning and organizing
 Role expertise
 

Job Requirements

#LI-EG1

Qualifications:
 Master’s Degree in operations research, applied statistics, data mining,
machine learning,
computer science or a related discipline.
 8+ years of experience delivering data science outcomes, solving complex
analytical problems
using quantitative approaches through a blend of analytical, mathematical and
technical skills.
 Understanding of statistical and predictive modeling concepts,
machine-learning approaches,
clustering and classification techniques, and recommendation and optimization
algorithms.
 Desire to solve business problems and to find patterns and insights within
structured and
unstructured data.
 Expertise in analyzing large, complex, multi-dimensional datasets with a
variety of tools.
 Proficiency in the use of statistical analysis environments such as R, or an
analytical programming
language such as Python.
 Experience with data visualization tools such as ggplot2, matplotlib, or
D3.js
 Experience with BI tools such as Microstrategy is a plus.
 Comfortable with relational databases and SQL queries.
ADA Physical/Mental/Workplace Requirements:
 Physical Requirements: The employee frequently is required to sit, talk or
listen and drive long
distances. The employee is occasionally required to stand; stoop, kneel,
crouch, or crawl; and
taste or smell. Frequent walking, including going up and down stairs and
ladders, and bending are
required. The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 50 pounds.
 Work Environment: Working conditions in certain areas of the winery are cold,
wet, slippery and
noisy. Involves exposure to hazardous conditions. Irregular shifts, including
graveyard, and long
hours may be required, particularly during harvest.
 Travel Required: Up to 10%, domestic and international.
