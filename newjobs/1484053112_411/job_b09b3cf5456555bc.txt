Overview:

MDA Information Systems LLC has an immediate opening at a customer location for
a Geospatial All Source Analyst-Data Manager in McLean, VA. This is a full-time
customer-site position providing support to a US Government agency. 

 

The Company: MDA Information Systems LLC is a major supplier of geospatial
intelligence and weather information. The firm serves a diverse client base
including commercial and governmental organizations in agriculture, energy,
defense, and civilian government. MDA Information Systems LLC offers an
excellent comprehensive benefits package including medical, dental, life, and
short/long-term disability insurance, vacation and sick leave, 401(k)
retirement plan, Flexible Spending Account and tuition assistance. MDA
Information Systems LLC is an Equal Opportunity Employer.

  

General Summary

This position will develop, populate and analyze a database containing
geospatial data and documents. This position requires knowledge of available
government furnished information sources and how to effectively mine data from
these disparate sources into a single database. The candidate must have a
strong background in conducting all source and open source research and must be
able to adapt to a fluid set of requirements.

 

To be qualified you must be a US Citizen and must have an active TS/SCI
with Polygraph

Responsibilities:

  • Continually develop and populate a geospatial database based on changing
    customer requirements
  • Classify unstructured text into a structured database
  • Utilize government furnished gazetteers and other resources to derive
    coordinates from text
  • Provide analysts with data and trends from the database in support of
    regular and ad hoc analytic products
  • Perform data acquisition from multiple sources

Qualifications:

Required Qualifications:

  • Extensive experience using Excel including charts, complex formulas and
    Pivot Tables
  • Experience using ArcGIS to ingest tabular data and creating choropleth or
    density maps
  • Experience working in a fast paced office with the ability to work with
    little or no supervision and adapt to an evolving set of requirements
  • Bachelor's Degree

Desired Qualifications:

  • Experience with Excel functions and/or scripting languages for process
    automation
  • Experience administering databases
  • Strong cartographic and/or graphic design skills
  • Experience creating and updating relational databases in Oracle or other
    common database formats
