   Title            Statistician - Business Analytics

   City             Indianapolis

   State / Province Indiana

   Country          USA

   Workplace        Local
   Arrangement

   Company Overview At Lilly, we unite caring with discovery to make life
                    better for people around the world. We are a global
                    healthcare leader headquartered in Indianapolis,
                    Indiana. Our 39,000 employees around the world work to
                    discover and bring life-changing medicines to those who
                    need them, improve the understanding and management of
                    disease, and give back to our communities through
                    philanthropy and volunteerism. We give our best effort
                    to our work, and we put people first. We?re looking for
                    people who are determined to make life better for
                    people around the world.

   Responsibilities Our customers and their needs continue to
                    evolve. Understanding the effect of each interaction
                    they may have with Lilly (touchpoints) is essential to
                    improving their experience and our resource
                    allocation. Part of the responsibility of the Business
                    Analytics team includes understanding our customers?
                    experience, their touchpoints, organizing our
                    information about these touchpoints, and appropriately
                    applying measurement and analytics to inform our
                    decision making process.

                    As part of the Business Analytics Team this research
                    scientist will provide analytical support for marketing
                    and sales related projects or finance projects. He/she
                    is expected to identify and establish relevant core
                    methodologies and approaches with appropriate data
                    requirements. This role will report into the leader of
                    Business Analytics within Global Statistical Sciences/
                    Advanced Analytics.
                     
                    This opening is for a skilled, trained statistician
                    with an emphasis on data mining and modeling. We are
                    especially interested in persons with expertise in
                    developing and optimizing business analytic
                    models. Successful candidates are expected to work with
                    multi-disciplinary teams to solve challenging problems
                    of importance to commercialization.
                     
                    The job tasks listed below outline the scope of the
                    position. The application of these tasks may vary,
                    based upon current business needs. 

                      * The candidate must demonstrate strong analytical
                        skills with business acumen
                      * To employ statistical modeling techniques and
                        generate tools to answer business questions
                        especially to support promotion response modeling;
                        marketing mix optimization; resource allocation
                        across diverse personal promotional channels; and
                        segmentation
                      * To be fully grounded in Lilly?s sales operations
                        data sources and able to respond to data requests
                        from business partners
                      * Have broad understanding about sales and marketing 
                        and be fully engaged with business teams, bringing
                        an objective voice to the table, and facilitating
                        decisions grounded in data
                      * To work closely with business partners to
                        understand key business decisions and the
                        information they require in order to make those
                        decisions
                      * To identify existing resources that answer teams?
                        business questions without the need for acquiring
                        new data
                      * To oversee technical methods done by  vendors
                      * To influence strategic priorities and resource
                        allocation during the business planning process 
                      * Stay current with respect to statistical/
                        mathematical/informatics modeling methodology, to
                        maintain proficiency in applying new and varied
                        methods, and to be competent in justifying methods
                        selected
                      * Participate in peer-review work products and advise
                        others on technical matters
                      * Can effectively lead cross-functional or
                        multi-disciplinary groups and use technical
                        expertise to influence business decisions through
                        statistical/mathematical analysis and
                        interpretation.
                      * Perform work in full compliance with assigned
                        curriculum(s) and will be responsible for following
                        applicable corporate, medical, local, and
                        departmental policies, procedures, processes, and
                        training
                    
   Basic              * Master's degree in Statistics, Biostatistics, or
   Qualifications       Economics
                      * Qualified candidates must be legally authorized to
                        be employed in the United States. Lilly does not

                        visa status (e.g., H-1B or TN status) for this
                        employment position.
                    
   Additional         * Preferred experience in marketing and sales area or
   Skills/              finance
   Preferences        * Deep and broad knowledge of statistical modeling
                        and data mining methods and tools ? able to reduce
                        business problems to mathematical/statistical
                        questions/hypotheses that can be answered through
                        analytics and data mining
                      * Proficiency with analytic tools (SAS, JMP, R etc.)
                      * Interpersonal communication skills for effective
                        customer consultation
                      * Teamwork and leadership skills
                      * Strong learning agility
                      * Self-management skills with a focus on results for
                        timely and accurate completion of competing
                        deliverables
                      * Insatiable curiosity for understanding existing
                        information sources, uncovering new ones, and
                        developing and improving on analysis approaches
                      * Demonstrated problem solving ability and strategic
                        thinking
                      * Positive and professional approach
                      * Detail oriented
                      * Ability to do prioritization across multiple
                        projects
