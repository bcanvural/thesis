Data Scientist

  • Goleta, CA
  • Full Time
  • Marketing
  • Experienced

  •  
  •  
  •  

InTouch Health is the leading telehealth solution company enabling specialist
physician care anytime, anywhere.  We are looking for an eager, self-starting
Data Scientist to join our team! This position will help us discover the
information that is hidden in the vast amounts of data that we generate within
our marketing organization and help us make smarter decisions about the
marketing efforts that we undertake. The primary focus will be in applying data
mining techniques, doing statistical analysis, and building high-quality
prediction systems integrated across our marketing technology stack. The
individual will have a deep understanding of web analytics, attribution, and
tracking methodologies, and a working knowledge of digital marketing campaigns.
This role will report to the Director of Revenue Marketing and work closely
with marketing managers to report on performance. A successful candidate in
this role will be able to see deep into the numbers, but also provide
comprehensive dashboards and reports that are easily digestible at all levels
of the organization. 

Responsibilities

  • Work with the operations team to support tracking, reporting, and
    attribution methodology across marketing
  • Develop reporting and dashboards to answer key business questions and
    connect the dots around performance and brand marketing
  • Understand the technology and tracking of programs that support the
    analytics and be able to help with troubleshooting
  • Own weekly, monthly and quarterly performance reporting - both total funnel
    analysis and per program analysis
  • Mine data from multiple sources and manipulate it into an easily
    understandable format
  • Assist with budget tracking and reporting
  • Analyze KPIs such as leads, pipeline, and bookings by various marketing
    channels, lead segments, campaigns, timeframes and sales channels
  • Work with marketing stakeholders to streamline analyses across multiple
    marketing channels, including: email, SEM, SEO, content, display, events
    and branding in order to ensure ROI of our marketing programs spend
    investments
  • Determine the set of tools, automation, and processes needed to scale with
    the business
  • Complete other duties as assigned

Knowledge, Skills & Ability

  • Expert Excel skills (Can build a slick model without touching the mouse)
  • Experience with common data science toolkits, such as R, Weka, NumPy,
    MatLab, etc…
  • Experience with data visualization tools, such as D3.js, GGplot, etc…
  • Proficiency in using query languages, such as SQL, Hive, Pig, etc…
  • Thorough understanding of the digital marketing ecosystem and marketing
    analytics best practices
  • Proven track record of using data to generate insight and drive business
    results in areas such as attribution, brand awareness, event marketing, and
    product marketing
  • Fluency in data analysis and communication about data, including time
    series analysis, campaign analysis, data visualization, and defining KPI
    strategies for marketing
  • Good applied statistics skills, such as distributions, statistical testing
    and regression
  • Data-oriented personality
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Assertive, tenacious and driven to succeed
  • Strong work ethic and result-oriented
  • Ability to multi-task and work in fast paced environment and function
    through ambiguity
  • Works well with team members and cross-departmentally

Qualifications

  • Master’s Degree in related field (e.g., research, applied mathematics,
    statistics, data mining)
  • 5 – 7 years of experience in data science and analytics roles
  • Marketing Automation Platform experience (Marketo preferred)
  • Deep understanding of CRM tool (Salesforce preferred)
  • Experience in analytics tool required (such as DOMO or Tableau)
  • Exposure to Marketo Revenue Cycle Explorer a plus

Working Conditions

  • Normal office with long periods working at a computer
  • Must be able to lift up to 40 lbs. unassisted
  • 10% travel

Notes

For the qualified candidate, InTouch Health offers competitive compensation,
medical, dental and vision benefits, paid time off, 401(K) and stock options.
InTouch Health is based in sunny and beautiful Santa Barbara, California.
