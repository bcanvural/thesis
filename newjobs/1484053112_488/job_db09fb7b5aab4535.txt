  Role Summary:
  Data Scientist will work in teams addressing statistical, machine learning
  and data understanding problems in a technology development environment.
  You will contribute to the development and deployment of modern machine
  learning, operational research, semantic analysis, and statistical methods
  for finding structure in large data sets. As a Data Scientist, you will be
  part of a cross-disciplinary team on solution and product development
  projects, typically involving large, complex data sets.

  Essential Responsibilities:

    • Develop analytics to address data science needs and opportunities
    • Translate algorithms into tools to be incorporated into GE products and
      services
    • Be responsible as part of technical teams for development, deployment,
      and application of applied analytics, predictive analytics, and
      prescriptive analytics
    • Perform exploratory and targeted data analyses using descriptive
      statistics and other methods
    • Conduct data quality assessments, data cleansing and data analytics
    • Generate reports, annotated code, and other projects artifacts to
      document, archive, and communicate your work and outcomes. Share and
      discuss findings with team members.



  Qualifications/Requirements:

    • Minimum Master’s Degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Machine Learning,
      Materials Science or a related field
    • Minimum 1 year analytics development experience with high-level
      languages, such as R, Python, Perl, Ruby or similar scripting languages
    • Minimum 1 year hands-on experience in sourcing, cleaning, manipulating
      and analyzing large volumes of data
    • Minimum 1 year hands-on experience in quantitative image analysis
    • Must be willing to relocated to Pittsburgh, PA area.

  Legal
  authorization to work in the U.S. is required; we will not sponsor
  individuals
  for employment visas, now or in the future.


  Desired Characteristics:

    • Experience with additive manufacturing
    • Experience with point cloud manipulation software such as PolyWorks and
      Volume Graphics
    • Demonstrated awareness of feature extraction and current analytics
      methods
    • Demonstrated awareness of analytic prototyping, analytic scale up,
      analytic scaling, and solutions integration
    • Proficiency in statistical analysis, quantitative analytics,
      visualization, forecasting/predictive analytics, multivariate testing,   
      and optimization algorithms
    • Independent thinker and worker who can operate with little direction,
      but is still comfortable working in a collaborative environment.
      •
    • High level of precision and logical thinking
    • Strong problem solving and organization skills
    • Ability to accept new challenges
    • Strong oral and written communication skills
    • Strong interpersonal and leadership skills
