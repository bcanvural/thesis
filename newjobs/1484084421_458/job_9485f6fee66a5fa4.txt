Description

GumGum seeks a full time Data Scientist at our offices located at 1314 7th
Street, 5th Floor, Santa Monica, CA 90401 to perform the following duties:

  • Own the design, specifications, implementation and quality control of
    context-gathering algorithms (R&D and production);
  • Communicate with end user to understand business needs and design systems
    to meet those requirements;
  • Use machine learning classification algorithms on pages to understand
    content and brand safety; improve ad targeting capabilities;
  • Discover trends and ad server revenue optimization opportunities using
    custom algorithms;
  • Fraud detection using machine learning; and
  • Keyword extraction and entity identification.

Minimum Education and Experience Requirements: Master's degree, or foreign
equivalent, in Computer Science, or related academic field, plus one (1) year
of experience in the Job Offered, Software Engineer, or related IT position. 

Special Requirements: Knowledge, which may be gained concurrently, pre- or
post-degree and through coursework or experience, to include each of the
following: 1) natural language processing and machine learning; 2) Programming
with Python; 3) Statistical natural language processing; and 4) Advanced Data
Analytics techniques. Must have excellent written and spoken communications
skills.
