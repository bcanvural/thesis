Job Opening: Data Scientist, Strike Social

Company background:

Strike Social develops artificial intelligence and provides 24/7 optimization
to drive the best outcomes in social media advertising. Through media-buying
software and service, Strike powers 15,000+ campaigns globally each day for the
world?s leading agencies and brands, including Disney, Honda, Xbox, Mattel and
Wendy?s. With global operations, Strike is headquartered in Chicago and was
named the city's Best B2B Startup in the 2016 Moxie Awards.

Job Responsibilities:

  * Design and implement ETL processes.
  * Play a key role architecting, building, and scaling our private data
    warehouse.
  * Design and implement data access and retention policies.
  * Partner with product development and data science teams to research,
    design, and implement a closed?-loop learning system.
  * Report progress, issues, and status at daily and weekly stand-ups.
  * Stay abreast of emerging technologies and new design approaches.
  * Provide technical leadership both within and outside the development team.

Qualifications:

  * Bachelor?s degree or higher - Computer Science or equivalent field
  * 3+ years of Data Engineering / Software Engineering and designing software
    solutions for high availability systems.
  * 2+ years experience working in an object oriented programming language
    (Python, Ruby, Java, C#, C++)
  * 2+ years experience working with both RDBMS and NoSQL.
  * 1+ years experience working in a cloud based deployment environment.
  * Agile software development methodology experience
  * Take pride in writing clean, well tested, and dependable code
  * Strong ability to analyze problems and formulate effective solutions
  * Sharp critical thinking skills, including systems and business analysis,
    problem analysis and resolution, and sound judgment/decision making ability
  * Proven ability to work in a rapid release product environment
  * Experience working with social advertising apis (Google adwords, Facebook,
    Instagram, Linkedin, and/or Snapchat) is a plus


Working at Strike Social:

Strike Social is a close-knit, work-hard/play-hard environment. If you want a

standard (and generous) employee benefits, Strike offers unlimited PTO, an open
office environment, casual dress code, a spectacular Loop location and frequent
team outings.
