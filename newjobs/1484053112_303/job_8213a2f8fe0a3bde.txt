Federal - Data Scientist

Job Location: VA - Chantilly

Job Number: 00435216 00435216_en

APPLY GET REFERRED SAVE THIS JOB

- Job description

Organization: Accenture Federal Services

Location: Chantilly, VA

                                      

Great outcomes are everything. It's what drives us to turn bold ideas into
breakthrough solutions that solve the toughest problems fast--the first time.
So you can change how people work and live.


Responsibilities:

  • Designs, develops, build classifiers and implements quantitative models.
    Performs exploratory data analysis to identify attributes and apply
    predictive analytics techniques including information retrieval, machine
    learning, network analysis and data mining Researches, tests, builds, and
    performs data transformations for data reduction and variable analysis of
    risk analysis to implement statistical techniques. 
  • Designs and develops new models or refresh to existing software.
  • Collaborates with project teams and stakeholders to identify client
    requirements.
  • Performs reporting analysis on modeling gains and impact analysis.
  • Implements modeling features and methods to develop custom architectures
    and application integration for program execution.
  • Thorough data analyses and predictive modeling to answer complex business
    requirements like marketing Return-on-Investment and Future Employee Hiring
    Demand.
  • Creates meaningful and rich data visualizations that will excite the client
    and provide insight into their business problems
  • Presents products in client meetings and lead client discussions.

 

+ Basic qualifications

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree required (Computer Science, Engineering, or Statistics)
    with 7+ years of work experience in a technical field OR Master’s Degree
    required (Computer Science, Engineering, or Statistics) with 5+ years of
    work experience in a technical field.
  • 3+ years of experience in data collection, exploratory data analysis, and
    statistical modeling.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Programming Skills: R, SAS, MDX, Matlab, Python, Java or C++.
  • Data Modeling and Services Experience using either SQL or NoSQL databases.
  • Six Sigma Certification.
  • Excel Guru
  • AWS Certified
  • Strong multi-tasking and communication skills.
  • Results oriented and self-motivated.

An active security clearance or the ability to obtain one may be required for
this role.

 

Candidates who are currently employed by a client of Accenture or an affiliated
Accenture business may not be eligible for consideration.

 

Applicants for employment in the U.S. must possess work authorization which
does not require now or in the future sponsorship by the employer for a visa.

US Citizenship Required.
