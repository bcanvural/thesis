What is it Like Working at the World’s Largest Winery?

Recently recognized by Glassdoor as one of the “Top 10 Companies for
Outstanding Work Culture”, we couldn’t be prouder to call our team family. We
wouldn’t be where we are without them. At the heart of our success story, and
helping us to build over 80 award-winning brands, is our research and
development team. We make it our top priority to empower them with the freedom
of innovation that comes with limitless leadership. When you work for Gallo,
you can expect to be taken care of like family. You’ll enjoy a positive
work-life balance, an intuitive powerhouse of support, and robust benefits that
far exceed the standards of most corporations.

 

Pairing Your Statistics Expertise with Gallo Winery

The Statistician for our research and development team plays a unique role in
moving the science and engineering of viticulture and wine making forward. As
part of this innovative team of researchers from a wide variety of disciplines,
you will analyze agriculture, biological, chemical and engineering data in
order to formulate forward-thinking strategies. Your research will touch many
aspects of our business, both inside and out. Your will conduct scientific
research focused on everything from grape growing to winemaking , as well as
production technology to help shape and implement research strategies and
transform them into visionary recommendations.

 

Essential Functions

 

Research Strategy and Statistical Analysis

  • Use experimental and research design strategies in the design and execution
    of research strategies that support R&D programs
  • Evaluate key platforms by independently performing data analysis/
    segmentation using the following methods:

ANOVA, regression based modeling, mixed modeling, partial least squares,
structural equation modeling, attribution modeling, machine learning, etc.

  • Understand data sets that include spatial and/or temporal auto-correlation
    with non-normal data
  • Based on the results of the research, make recommendations to the business
    for ways to continually improve strategies to address corporate objectives

 

Data Management and Reporting

  • Be part of a team monitoring our data ecosystem, helping us evaluate new
    application and providing recommendations for current data collection tools
    and sources
  • Monitor how changes to metric definition and data sources might impact
    current research and modeling strategies and approaches
  • Partner with a variety of our cross-functional partners such as
    viticulture, chemistry, genetics, enology, process technology, grape
    supply, and grower relations, to execute, interpret, and apply research
    results
  • Lead the development and implementation of reporting processes and
    dashboard platforms and communicate scientific findings to field
    practitioners, enologists, chemists, and other members of the broader
    organization

 

 

Experience We’re Looking for:

  • Ph.D. in a Quantitative Discipline, OR
  • Master of Science Degree in a Quantitative Discipline plus 2 years of
    experience with advanced data analysis and statistics or marketing research
    showing growing levels of responsibility, OR
  •  Bachelor of Science in a Quantitative Discipline plus 4 years of 
    experience with advanced data analysis and statistics or marketing research
    reflecting growing levels of responsibility

What We’d Prefer:

  • Experience with statistical software such as SPSS, SAS, R, and/or The
    Unscrambler
  • Database modeling or programming experience with SQL, R or Python
  • Comfortable working with large data sets and computationally intensive
    analyses using R and Neteeza
  • BS, MS, Ph.D. in research sciences (biology, bioinformatics, chemistry,
    hydrology, soil, genetics, botany, microbiology, etc.)
  • Demonstrated experience defining and executing research strategies and
    programs
  • Strong data visualization skills
  • Agriculture and/or wine industry experience

 

What Makes a Great Pairing?

If you are passionate about viticulture and have a nose for finding great
opportunities, you’ll feel right at home with Gallo. Here, you will find the
opportunity to become an owner and inventor; a leader and a team member.
Develop skills and learn, knowing you’ll have the stability of working with an
industry leader. We will never put limits on your innovative pursuits and take
pride in offering our staff top-notch benefits. A career with Gallo truly is a
full-bodied experience. 

 

Gallo does not sponsor for employment based visas for this position now or in
the future
