Description

Wright Avenue Partners is building a team of top notch statistical talent who
are looking to improve their careers with flexible and challenging work
assignments. We’re currently seeking a Principal Statistician to lead a team of
statisticians and programmers to manipulate, summarize and analyze clinical
trial data using a variety of statistical methods, ultimately delivering
clinical trial results to an agreed timeline with optimal quality. If you’d
like to join our team in RTP, North Carolina, submit your application below.

Responsibilities

As a Principal Statistician you will perform the following tasks:

  • Project management responsibilities, including allocating staff, predicting
    and planning resources
  • Prepare and review study documentation e.g. protocols and statistical
    analysis plans
  • Advise, mentor and teach both internal and external statisticians with
    regards to statistical and data analysis methods
  • Function as a study expert across a number of studies, helping to clarify
    details on analysis methods for the internal team
  • Ensure the team meets the highest quality standards, and follows the
    principles detailed in internal procedures
  • Maintain a positive and engaging client relationship with regards to
    statistical issues

Qualifications

  • PhD or MSc in Biostatistics or related discipline
  • Experience working within a clinical trials environment (Pharma, CRO or
    academia)
  • Previous experience working with SAS
  • Excellent written and verbal communication
  • Ability to communicate with a broad range of people across different
    functions
