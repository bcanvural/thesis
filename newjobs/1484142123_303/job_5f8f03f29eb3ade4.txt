JOB SUMMARY:

Using clinical, business and external data, along with advanced modeling and
data mining techniques, develop actionable insights that drive improvements in
patient care and reduce costs. Specifically, produce forecasting models and
recommender systems which can be used to optimize care, manage risk (both
clinical and financial), and support population health initiatives. This is a
system-wide staff position, which supports Mission's programs and services
through expertise in Statistics, Econometrics, Applied Mathematics, Decision
Science, Industrial Engineering, and/or Operations Research.

 

 

REQUIRED EDUCATION:  Master's Degree in Statistics, Biostatistics,
Econometrics, Applied Mathematics, Decision Science, Industrial Engineering, or
related field

 

 

PREFERRED EDUCATION:  Ph.D. in Statistics, Biostatistics, Econometrics, Applied
Mathematics, Decision Science, Industrial Engineering, or related field with a
similar quantitative orientation.

 

 

REQUIRED EXPERIENCE:  Strong mathematical modeling and data mining skills.
Demonstrate proficiency with statistical modeling, including working knowledge
of statistical methods and techniques such as regression (OLS, GLM, ARIMA,
LASSO and variants, etc.) and machine learning (deep learning, SVM, tree-based
methods, etc.). Advanced R programming proficiency required; proficiency in
Python, JavaScript, and SAS a plus. Proficiency with SQL is required. Aptitude
and willingness to learn new analytical methods, software applications, and
tools. Demonstrate strong written, verbal communication, and presentation
skills. Demonstrate strong organizational and time management skills, with
attention to detail. Demonstrate strong partnering and relationship-building
skills.

 

 

PREFERRED EXPERIENCE:  Two or more years of equivalent/related professional or
academic experience in applications of advanced analytics in production-level
systems preferably in the health services industry Experience with SQL
programming. Demonstrated strong leadership skills. Knowledge of statistical
modeling techniques encountered in a healthcare context, and ability to provide
practical interpretation of model results for non-expert audiences . Experience
with advanced data visualization tools (e.g. QlikView, Tableau, TIBCO
Spotfire).
