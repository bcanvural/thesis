Date: Dec 21, 2016

Location: Raleigh, NC, US

Company: Cisco Systems, Inc.

Additional Location(s) or Information: Research Triangle Park

Job Category: Business Strategy

Level of Experience: Experienced - Non Manager

Requisition #: R1025179

 

Description:

What You'll Do
The Operations Architecture team remains responsible for defining, developing,
and governing Cisco’s operating architecture and commercial delivery models,
including exciting new growth areas like XaaS and Software. This operations
architecture is the foundation by which we run our business.

You will:
● Provide Business insights that solve complex problems in a fast paced,
collaborative and iterative analytical environment.
● Coach and mentor other Data Scientists and Business Analysts
● Lead complex analytics projects, dividing work into series of questions to be
answered by the project team.
● Lead and practice secure and ethical usage of data to generate insights.
● Shape the future of Data Science practices by utilizing common frameworks
such as CRISP-DM.

Who You'll Work With
Business Intelligence & Analytics (BI&A) is a shared service organization
providing consistent, trusted data, leading-edge analytics, and actionable
enterprise insights. Our vision is to accelerate Cisco's competitive advantage
by enabling leaders to understand the past, manage the present, and change the
future through data-driven decision making. We are uniquely positioned to
contribute to Cisco's success, with innovative technical capabilities, deep
analytical skills, and cross functional business acumen on a single team.

Who You Are
● You are passionate about asking and answering questions in large datasets,
and you are able to communicate that passion to stakeholders.
● You are an expert in analyzing large, complex, multi-dimensional datasets
with a variety of tools.
● You are able to switch seamlessly between relational databases as you are
with Hadoop-based data mining frameworks.
● You have a deep understanding of statistical and predictive modeling
concepts, machine-learning approaches, clustering and classification
techniques, and recommendation and optimization algorithms.

Our Minimum Requirements for this role are;
● 3 Years’ experience in delivering Data Science solution in Finance, Economics
or Computer Science
● Experience with SQL, Python, Java, Scala or C/C++
● Experience using statistical analysis environments such as R, MATLAB, SPSS or
SAS.
●Bachelor's Degree in Finance, Economics, or Computer Science.
● Preferred - Master’s Degree in operations research, applied statistics, data
mining, machine learning, physics or a related quantitative discipline

Why Cisco
We connect everything: people, processes, data, and things. We innovate
everywhere, taking bold risks to shape the technologies that give us smart
cities, connected cars, and handheld hospitals. And we do it in style with
unique personalities who aren’t afraid to change the way the world works,
lives, plays and learns.

We are thought leaders, tech geeks, pop culture aficionados, and we even have a
few purple haired rock stars. We celebrate the creativity and diversity that
fuels our innovation. We are dreamers and we are doers.

We Are Cisco.
