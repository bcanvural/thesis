Data Scientist
Location: New York City
Reports To: Director, Engineering

Our Company:

DoubleVerify is the recognized market innovator with the technology and tools
that accurately authenticate the quality of digital media and drive ad
performance for the world's largest brands. DV provides media transparency and
accountability to deliver the highest level of impression quality for maximum
advertising performance. Since 2008, DV has helped hundreds of Fortune 500
companies gain the most from their media spend by delivering best in class
solutions across the digital ecosystem that help build a better industry. Learn
more at doubleverify.com.

Position Overview

As a Data Scientist at DV you will be a part of a highly motivated and talented
team delivering creative statistical solutions for a variety of challenging
problems such as  identifying machines infected by malware, detecting automated
bots mimicking human users, developing targeting segments for real time bidding
environments, detecting fraudulent sites.

You will be responsible for analyzing terabytes of daily data, creating state
of the art visualizations, building statistical models, developing machine
learning systems capable of processing billions of events a day and producing
insights that drive DV’s business forward. You will work closely with the
product team to successfully deliver DV’s product roadmap.

What you will do:

  • Build statistical models for various problems including: projections,
    clustering, classification, sampling, fraud detection, anomaly detection,
    etc.
  • Conduct researches and perform POCs involving analysis of terabytes of data
  • Come up with superb visualizations and illustrations of data
  • Develop Machine Learning systems working along side with DV’s production
    environment
  • Write production quality code while implementing your own ideas

What you have done / who you are:

  • MS in Statistics, Mathematics or related field
  • Strong analytical thinker
  • Hands-on experience in analysis and modeling of complex data sets
  • Expert level in R, Matlab or a similar environment
  • Experience with a programming language (e.g Python)
  • Experience working with 'Big Data' using technologies such as SQL, Hive/
    Hadoop, Spark is a plus
