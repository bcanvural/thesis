About Conversion Logic

Conversion Logic is a next generation cross channel marketing attribution
measurement and optimization platform.  This advanced enterprise attribution
SaaS platform utilizes machine-learning algorithms, emphasizes automation, data
driven accuracy, an elegant UI and velocity to value.  Drawing on deep domain
area expertise, Conversion Logic is committed to simple, timely and actionable
insights, surpassing all competitors in the market today by utilizing the
state-of-the-art in technology and engineering infrastructure. 

 

Data Science Team at Conversion Logic

Conversion Logic’s Data Science team consists of world class Data Scientists.
The team won 1st prize out of 800+ teams at KDD Cup 2015 and finished 4th place
out of 500+ teams at ECML/PKDD Challenge 2016. 

Our Data Science team leveraged the award winning Ensemble approach in
Conversion Logic’s products to innovate in the marketing attribution market,
where Linear Regression has been a dominant approach.

Besides day-to-day work, the team participates in international Data Science
competitions with world-class data scientists across the world on a regular
basis, which has been tremendous fun as well as a great learning and networking
opportunity to the team.

 

Role

As a Data Scientist, you will be part of our award winning Data Science team
that is responsible for R&D in advanced attribution modeling and analytical
support to client service and engineering teams in client delivery and product
development.  The candidate must be a strong communicator and influencer with a
track record of delivering on-time concurrent projects in a fast
paced environment.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

·       Research, develop, operate, and extend advanced marketing attribution
models with Machine Learning algorithms to be deployed in production

·       Collaborate closely with client services and engineering to deliver
insights to clients on time

·       Define and implement features to our marketing analytics products in
Python

 

REQUIREMENTS

·       Degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Physics, or
Engineering

·       Experience in predictive modeling with Machine Learning algorithms

·       Understanding of probability, statistics, and linear algebra

·       Solid coding skills in Python

·       Excellent teamwork and communication ability

·       Being passionate in Data Science, disciplined at work, and confident
while being humble
