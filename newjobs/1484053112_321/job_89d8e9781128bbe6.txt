Using data + predictive modeling, our mission at Impact Health is to make
buying health insurance online simple and transparent. We are a venture-backed
startup obsessively focused on our customer. Using machine learning and
artificial intelligence, we are personalizing healthcare for the consumer.

Our people are more than just resources, they are the heart of our company. We
are building the best, most talented, and happiest team possible.

Data Scientist

  • Playa del Rey, CA
  • Full Time
  • Data Science
  • Experienced

  •  
  •  
  •  

 

We are looking for an ambitious Data Scientist to join our team in Los Angeles.

THE JOB 
You'll be taking complex healthcare data to develop predictive models and
algorithms that will help people all over the US get health insurance they can
actually use. Data scientists will refine and invent ways to predict the best
search results for health insurance consumers.

This is a great opportunity to leverage your skills to have a huge impact on
the world. Your technical and imaginative genius will directly help consumers
across the U.S. get health insurance they can actually use.

YOU LIKELY HAVE: 

  • A deep desire to build a high quality product and company. 
  • 3+ years experience as a Data Scientist, Software Engineer with predictive
    modeling, or similar 
  • PhD or Master’s Degree in applied statistics, data mining, machine
    learning, or a related quantitative discipline. 
  • Proficiency in NLP, predictive modeling, algorithms, machine learning and a
    huge imagination. 
  • Top notch programming skills in Java and/or Python 
  • Extremely comfortable working with Big Data 
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills. We will count on you to
    be a data storyteller. 
  • A strong desire to work in a fast-paced, high-growth environment 
  • An unstoppable passion to do the best work of your life

Our product & engineering team work out of our Playa del Rey, Ca office.
