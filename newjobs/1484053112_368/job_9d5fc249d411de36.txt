Date: Jan 10, 2017

Location: Palo Alto, CA, US

Company: SAP

Requisition ID: 132417
Work Area: Software-Design and Development
Expected Travel: 0 - 10%
Career Status: Professional
Employment Type: Regular Full Time

 

COMPANY DESCRIPTION

 

As market leader in enterprise application software, SAP helps companies of all
sizes and industries innovate through simplification. From the back office to
the boardroom, warehouse to storefront, on premise to cloud, desktop to mobile
device – SAP empowers people and organizations to work together more
efficiently and use business insight more effectively to stay ahead of the
competition. SAP applications and services enable customers to operate
profitably, adapt continuously, and grow sustainably.

 

Our SAP Innovation Center Silicon Valley creates technology prototypes with
strategic customers, start-ups and leading research institutions. The team is
built with rock stars from universities from around the globe and exploring
technologies around natural user interfaces (face, voice, speaker, gesture
recognition), machine learning, AR, VR, the Internet of Things and full-stack
cloud applications.
 

In general a Data Scientist can work in different areas, for example in
customer projects (co-innovation, consulting, custom development, projects with
internal customers), in development or research.
 

Below you can find main key areas of responsibilities and tasks for Data
Scientists in different work areas:

* Customer projects: A Data Scientist working in customer projects executes the
mathematical modeling of our customers’ business problems, develops algorithms,
data mining flows and applications to solve these problems, and helps our
customers directly to understand and implement the results of the analysis. In
this role, the Data Scientist works directly with industry experts and
technology experts to translate our customers’ business needs into mathematical
models and present and deliver a solution to the client.
 

*Development: A Data Scientist working in development integrates or implements
data mining/ mathematical methods into products. Is responsible for evaluating
and testing data mining methods and maintains a high level of quality. Is
responsible to work closely with the senior data scientist to prototype new
innovations and eventually get them into the product.
Contributes to data definitions, data storage infrastructure for data mining
purposes and to Data Mining architectures. Resolves data mining performance
issues and monitors data mining system performance and implements efficiency
improvements. Implements data mining algorithms if required. Works closely with
product management, application developers, information developers and end
users.
 

*Research Projects: A Data Scientist working in research projects performs
functional and empirical analysis on data mining topics. Conducts research on
data mining tools and algorithms, implements prototypes and data mining
algorithms and validates them on real data. Contributes to best practices for
data mining topics and collaborates with development, universities and research
institutes.
 

WORK EXPERIENCE:
*Software development experience
*Hands-on experience with Data Mining tools
*Business English: Professional Working Proficiency
 

EDUCATION:
*Bachelor or Master degree in Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science or
equivalent education.
