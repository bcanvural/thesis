Data Scientist-Analytics Healthcare Platform

Job Description

Please be informed that Intel is proactively trying to find candidates for a 
Data Scientist position and that this position may not be available at this
time

Advanced Analytics is a competency center for machine learning & big data at
Intel. We deliver internal and external solutions/products that can create a
competitive advantage for Intel. 
We are looking for a talented Data Scientist, to join our data science team in
a unique & fascinating effort in the healthcare domain. 
In this effort, we are developing a cutting edge platform which leverages
wearable, mobile devices and big data cloud analytics technologies. The
platform aims to promote research and drug development in various chronic
diseases.

The job scope includes analysis of time series data using different machine
learning, deep learning and signal processing methods, in support of various
customers



Qualifications
MSc/PhD in Computer science/ Software engineering/Information technology/
Industrial Eng. Academic experience with Machine Learning algorithms during
studies Proven practical experience with Machine Learning algorithms and
techniques Strong analytical skills and knowledge in statistics and data
analysis Proven experience in R/Python Proven experience in signal processing
