Junior Data Scientist

  • CHICAGO, IL
  • Full Time
  • REVERB.COM, LLC
  • Entry Level

  •  
  •  
  •  

Reverb.com is looking for a Jr. Data Scientist to continue to enhance business
processes across our organization.  He or she will identify opportunities for
improvements through data-driven decision making.  

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with product development, marketing and finance teams to
    execute on data science roadmap.
  • Gather necessary data from internal stakeholders and external services/
    providers.
  • Proactively conduct research to identify areas of improvement and apply
    that knowledge across the organization.
  • Share insights into roadblocks or inefficiencies in data across teams and
    educate teams on best practices and aid in future growth.
  • Build and manage reporting dashboards for departments across the company.
  • Conduct ad-hoc data analysis to evaluate and provide recommendations for
    product, marketing, and other areas of the company.

Requirements:

  • Analytical background in statistics, economics, machine learning, physics,
    engineering, or computer science.  Bachelor’s degree required.
  • Demonstrated capabilities using applied statistics or machine learning in a
    professional or other intensive problem-solving environment with complex
    datasets.
  • Experience with SQL -- comfort writing queries and interpreting complex
    queries.
  • Experience with Python, R, Matlab, Stata, SAS or a comparable data-related
    programming language.  Python strongly preferred.
  • Knack for identifying and correcting problems in imperfect data.
  • Curiosity and drive to constantly learn and teach others.
  • Strong written/verbal communication and presentation skills.

What You'll Get:

  • Competitive salary and stock options in a high growth company.
  • No-bureaucracy environment where ownership and initiative is valued.
  • Health insurance and a healthy work environment-- no 80 hour weeks.
  • 401k with 4% match.
  • Flexible vacation and sick days.
  • A MacBookPro, monitor, keyboard, mouse of your choice. Even a stand up desk
    if you want!
  • Discounts on music gear.
