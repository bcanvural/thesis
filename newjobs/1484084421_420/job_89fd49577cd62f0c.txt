Date: Jan 7, 2017

Location: Charlotte, NC, US, 28273

Company: Sealed Air Corporation

Requisition id: 572 

Additional Location(s): None

 

Sealed Air Corporation creates a world that feels, tastes and works better. In
2015, the Company generated revenue of approximately $7.0 billion by helping
our customers achieve their sustainability goals in the face of today’s biggest
social and environmental challenges. Our portfolio of widely recognized brands,
including Cryovac® brand food packaging solutions, Bubble Wrap® brand
cushioning and Diversey® cleaning and hygiene solutions, enables a safer and
less wasteful food supply chain, protects valuable goods shipped around the
world, and improves health through clean environments. Sealed Air has
approximately 23,000 employees who serve customers in 169 countries. To learn
more, visit www.SealedAir.com.

Sealed Air Corporation creates a world that feels, tastes and works better. In

Scope of This Position:

• Build machine learning models aimed towards unlocking value from data sources
• Work with divisional stakeholders to validate machine learning models 
• Build a workflow for on-going evaluation and refinement of machine learning
models deployed in digital products
• Develop KPIs for tracking, baselining and monetisation of machine learning
models developed

 

 

Job Description 

Responsibilities:

  • Build machine learning models using supervised and unsupervised modeling
    techniques
  • Working under the guidance of project leads, help manage team projects via
    task scheduling and tracking, issue management, status reporting, budget
    management, and stakeholder communication
  • Develop statistical, mathematical and predictive models using strategic
    business understanding to build the algorithms necessary to ask the right
    questions and find the right answers
  • Explore and examine data from multiple disparate sources in order to
    identify, provide reporting on, and analyze trends in the KPIs as well as
    regular reporting for assigned departments
  • Provide technical analysis to prepare specifications for visual analytics
    and reporting needs – assist with QA and testing of new dash boarding/
    widgets
  • Explore and examine innovative ways to optimize the ‘cost-to-analyze’
    though an intelligent application of machine learning toolsets

 

Qualifications 

Required:

  • Master’s Degree Required
  • 5-8 years of related experience
  • Data science architecture and role of machine learning
  • Machine learning
  • Supervised and unsupervised machine learning model trainings
