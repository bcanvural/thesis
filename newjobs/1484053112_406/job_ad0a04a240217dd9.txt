MediQuire is a big data analytics platform for ambulatory care clinics that
treat low-income patients in the United States. Please review our open
positions and website. We look forward to hearing from you. 

Data Scientist

  • New York City, NY
  • Full Time
  • Mid Level

  •  
  •  
  •  

Are you interested in products that make lives better for those most in need?
MediQuire provides a software solution to medical providers and payers to
better measure and improve the delivery and outcomes of clinical care. As a
Data Scientist, you will be at the forefront of the development and deployment
of MediQuire’s data-driven solutions that improve quality of clinical data and
identify patients with highest need for care.

Position:

As a Data Scientist, you will have direct impact on creating data processing
pipelines and predictive analytics tools that will have immediate impact on the
delivery and quality of care of some of the poorest patients in the United
States. You also have an essential role in creating analysis insights that will
inform and guide our product and engineering teams as well as influence
workflows on our clients’ side. You will be working with a national database of
Electronic Health Record data, claims data and external data on social
determinates of health from patients across 20+ states.

Main Responsibilities:

  • Design, implementation, and operationalization of solutions for healthcare
    delivery based on clinical, socio-economic, operational, and healthcare
    claims data. In this, you will have a unique opportunity to transform data
    into models and predictive analytics tools that prioritize patients in
    greatest need of care, as well as enable providers to manage the health of
    their patient population resulting in improved outcomes and higher quality
    of care.
  • As input to these solutions, you will also take part in developing
    innovative methods to collect data and extract information from
    heterogeneous sources building on our current product platform that already
    delivers clinical data insights superior to the traditional clinical
    informatics solutions.
  • As part of the analytics team, work closely with the product and
    engineering teams to assure optimal integration and deployment of our
    data-driven advanced analytics components into MediQuire’s offerings.
  • Translate our data into an asset: building insights around clinical
    outcomes, operational data, and tracking model performance; and insights
    that drive innovation in features, new products, and outreach to new
    clients.

Minimum Qualifications:

  • You have a Graduate degree in a relevant field (data science, computer
    science, machine learning, statistics).
  • At least 2 years of experience in a comparable role, or a lengthier strong
    experience and track of building data-driven solutions based on sound
    quantitative approaches.
  • You have a strong track record of developing and applying advanced methods
    for managing large and heterogeneous data sources, information extraction,
    predictive modeling and model validation. Preferably, you’ll have
    experience with free-text/natural language analysis or advanced methods for
    information extraction.
  • You have a continuous drive to crack challenging problems, and eager to
    find the best possible way to convert data into actionable information.
  • You love working in a team and continuously and effectively communicate
    your ideas and findings. You know how to use your tools to visualize data
    to tell stories to internal and external stakeholders.
  • You have advanced knowledge and a track of implemented solutions in at
    least one of R, Python, Java, Scala.
  • You have excellent understanding and have used databases to effectively
    store and manage large amounts of heterogeneous data.
  • Preferably, you will have working knowledge and experience utilizing and
    interfacing distributed computing frameworks such as Spark.
  • Experience working with healthcare data sets is a plus but not required

Personal Characteristics:

  • You are very organized, love making to-do lists, are flexible to
    re-prioritize on the fly
  • Hospitable and solution-oriented in nature
  • You write down your goals and measure them regularly
  • You love to win, more than you hate to lose
  • You have high expectations of yourself and those around you
  • You enjoy being part of a team and doing whatever it takes to help that
    team succeed

Location:
