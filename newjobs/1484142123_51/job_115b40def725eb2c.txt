Biomedical Data Scientist

Key Role:

Work as a bioinformatics analyst for Booz Allen Hamilton focused on technical
services that support research and development using biomedical, including
clinical, survey, and genomics data. Leverage bioinformatics software and
platforms to solve problems and analyze data and apply expertise in advanced
analytics, working with industry leading computing technologists and data
scientists who solve large

-scale, complex, and challenging business problems across the public and
private sectors. Design and develop data architecture and schemas to address
research problems, perform analysis, explain the analysis to clients, and
provide solutions to the problem with Data-driven products. Develop machine
learning, data mining, and statistical and graph

-based algorithms designed to analyze massive data sets and ensure the proper
implementation and usage of algorithms. Serve as a client interface and
participate in requirements gathering and analysis, process and systems
definition, data analysis, presentation of analysis to the clients, and
development of algorithm driven products and solutions. Work independently and
in teams, develop and maintain accurate laboratory records, and author standard
operating procedures, whitepapers, and technical reviews. This position is
located in Boston, MA.

Basic Qualifications:

-2+ years of experience in bioinformatics, clinical data management, or similar

-Experience with R and designing and implementing machine learning, data
mining, statistics, and graph algorithms

-Experience with database design and management systems, data management, and
organizations schemas

-Experience with Linux systems and programming using languages, including
Python, Perl, and Java, C++, and C#

-Knowledge of SNP genotyping and NGS technologies

-Ability to explain and present complex scientific concepts orally and in
writing

-Ability to function independently with limited oversight

-Ability to work well within a team environment and be flexible to meet
evolving client needs

-Ability to obtain a security clearance

-MS degree in Bioinformatics or Computational Biology

Additional Qualifications:

-Experience with publishing articles in peer

-reviewed publications

-Possession of excellent oral and written communication skills

-PhD degree in Bioinformatics, Computational Biology, or a related field
preferred

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to
meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information.
