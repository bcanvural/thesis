Items Analyst Senior / Metrics / Data analysis / Maintenance Data

Submit to Job(s)    
Req ID :
375090BR
Job Code/Title :
E5453:Items Analyst Sr
Job Description :
The candidate will be responsible for the oversight of the Javelin program
repair facility automated shop floor database system. The candidate will ensure
data integrity by developing processes that measure the accuracy of entries
performed by depot personnel. The candidate will develop metrics and report the
results to senior leadership on program. The candidate will serve as the depot
liaison to provide latest status on identified database issues and the
implementation of corrective action. The candidate will develop a database user
scorecard that will be used to measure and identify reoccurring data input
errors. The candidate will coordinate with depot leadership to implement the
necessary process/training to reduce data input errors. The candidate will
ensure data accuracy for hardware configuration, serialization, dates,
operational runtime, failure description, charge numbers, time charging, proper
sequence of part removal and installation, and ensure that all repair,
engineering, and mod templates are appropriately annotated with the latest
information required. The position is located in Los Angeles, California. Up to
1 month of training is required at the Orlando facility in Florida.

Basic Qualifications
High level MS Office skills, experience with metrics, data analysis, and IT
tools associated with data manipulation of maintenance data.

Desired skills
Computer science background
Configuration management experience
