Quantitative Analyst - Model Validation

Job code: 160002T9
Date of publication: 2017-01-06 18:11:45.0

Starting date
    2016-04-25 00:00:00.0
Job function
    Risks
Business unit
    SG CIB
Location
    New York
Job type
    Permanent contract

Environment

DEPARTMENT DESCRIPTION

Looking to have someone join our Market Risk Department of Soci?t? G?n?rale in
New York. She/he will be in charge of the Model Validation Team (5 persons at
Master or DEA/PhD levels), acting as second line of defense for Soci?t?
G?n?rale in New York. This independent team is responsible for validating
pricing and liquidity models.

Based in New York, the team will handle more particularly the validation of
products or models being specifically a matter of this perimeter. As such, She/
he will have to develop transverse skills considering the local specificity in
connection with the teams based in Paris.

The auditors will be a key element in the elaboration of technical
documentations to the attention of the US regulator  


Mission

MAIN ACCOUNTABILITIES
Liquidity models
The team is responsible for identifying the key liquidity risk factors of the
product and evaluates the soundness of the first line of defense (DFIN) model
choice. This highly quantitative role in connection includes:

  * Model validation: calibration of assumptions and independent validation of
    Liquidity and Capital models
  * Continuous challenging and review of models assumption adequacy

The auditors will be a key element in the elaboration of technical
documentations to the attention of the US regulator.



Profile

COMPETENCIES

This highly quantitative role, in strong connection, includes:

  * review of the conceptual soundness of model specification and assumptions
    including tests in stressed market conditions, correctness of
    implementation or robustness of numerical aspects
  * review of product-model adequacy in light of hedging strategies and market
    liquidity with studies of product sensitivities in various market
    conditions
  * proposal of alternative approach
  * implementation (C #) of a given model in the Model Validation library
    (independent from the Front Office pricing library)
  * strong and proactive interaction with traders, RD teams, risk managers and
    finance
  * audit documentation with pricing framework conditions / limitations
  * Strong ability with C#

 
PRIOR WORK EXPERIENCE

Required:

  * Valuable experience of 5-10 years in a similar position

 
EDUCATION

Required:

 

  * Graduated from a top tier engineering school with masters in financial
    mathematics

 
LANGUAGES

Required:

  * English

Plus:

  * French
