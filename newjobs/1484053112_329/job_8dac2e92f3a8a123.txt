Senior Data Warehouse Analyst

Submit to Job(s)    
Req ID :
376006BR_2
Job Code/Title :
E2175:Data Warehouse Analyst Sr Stf
Job Description :
The Senior Data Analyst is the detective of the data science team. Languages
like R, Python, SQL and C are fundamental. Just like the data scientist, the
skills and talents that are needed for this role are diverse and range the
entire spectrum of the data science process combined with a healthy desire to
be hands-on and figure-it-out.

If you eat, sleep and breathe data, this data analytics position may be for
you. In Mission Systems and Solutions, we’re passionate about data and the ways
it can help change the lives of our customers. Our analysts are responsible for
data mining and business intelligence. In this role, you will conduct advanced
analyses, custom reporting/dashboards, and standardize all data used for
developing customer solutions. We’re looking for a natural and innovative
problem solver who can’t stop their curious mind and has a desire to dig deep
for insights that answer our customer's business questions.

Basic Qualifications
Preferred Languages: R, Python, HTML, Javascript, C/C++, SQL

Experience with Math, Statistics and Machine Learning

Proven communications and presentation skills

Team player

Desired skills
Spreadsheet tools (e.g. excel)

Database systems (SQL and NO SQL based)

BA/BS in Mathematics, Decision Science or related field

An innovative, positive, and self-directed attitude.

Interested in finding solutions to problems or methodology for improving
customer deliverables

Ability to operate effectively in a customer-facing, multi-agency environment

Experience working on an analytical platform

Experience in quantitative analytics is a plus

As a leading technology innovation company, Lockheed Martin’s vast team works
with partners around the world to bring proven performance to our customers’
toughest challenges. Lockheed Martin has employees based in many states
throughout the U.S., and Internationally, with business locations in many
nations and territories.

Join us at Lockheed Martin, where we’re engineering a better tomorrow.
