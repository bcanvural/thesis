Description:

The Intelligence & Homeland Security Group has a current opening for an OPIR
Scientist


The candidate shall be responsible for supporting processing, exploitation and
dissemination of data from multiple sensors to include operations and
production support, producing OPIR phenomenology reports. Performs routine OPIR
reports. Will provide support to GEOINT analysis and operation support for data
analysis and analytic support. They are
Conduct research IR remote sensing experience, radiative transfer, and thermal
physics. Create new thermal models based on the newly created Structured
Background Estimation algorithms. Knowledge of PSF-based point source
extraction a plus. This position is collaborating with OPIR - Automated PSF
extraction position.


Qualifications:

- Active TS/SCI clearance
- Experience and Education Requirements: Greater than 15 years of experience
with Bachelor’s Degree, Master’s Degree and some post Master’s Degree course
work preferred (no experience substitution allowed for education requirement)
- Experience with processing and exploitation, dissemination of data from
multiple sources


Leidos Overview:
Leidos is a global science and technology solutions leader working to solve the
world’s toughest challenges in the defense, intelligence, homeland security,
civil, and health markets. The company’s 33,000 employees support vital
missions for government and commercial customers. Headquartered in Reston,
Virginia, Leidos reported pro forma annual revenues of approximately $10
billion for the fiscal year ended January 1, 2016 after giving effect to the
recently completed combination of Leidos with Lockheed Martin's Information
Systems & Global Solutions business (IS&GS). For more information, visit
www.Leidos.com. The company’s diverse employees support vital missions for
government and commercial customers. Qualified women, minorities, individuals
with disabilities and protected veterans are encouraged to apply. Leidos is an
Equal Opportunity Employer.
