Associate Statistical Scientist - Product Development Biometrics

South San Francisco, California
Job ID: 2967147437
Return to Search Results
 Apply For This Job  Download PDF

The Position

The Position:Genentech seeks statisticians with strong interest in drug
development for positions supporting clinical development activities. Teamwork,
effective communication and collaboration skills are essential. The Associate
Statistical Scientist serves as Biostatistics representative on
cross-functional early development project and study teams, and has primary
strategic and operational responsibility for the design, conduct, analysis and
reporting of clinical studies. Responsibilities:

  * Partners with senior biostatistics staff and cross-functional members to
    provide technical and strategic input into clinical development plans
  * As part of clinical teams, collaborates in the design, planning, conduct,
    reporting and regulatory submissions of clinical studies to meet project
    objectives, including exploratory project activities such as biomarkers and
    PK/PD analysis.
  * Collaborates with scientists and contributes to presentations/publications
  * Understands and apply business requirements and processes
  * Keeps abreast of new developments in statistics, drug development, and
    regulatory guidance through literature review, workshop attendance, etc.
  * Contributes to the biostatistics/scientific community by partnering with
    senior staff, mentoring junior colleagues, and participating in internal/
    external initiatives

Who You Are

Qualifications:

  * MSc or PhD in statistics/biostatistics or a related field, with
    relevantexperience
  * Sound knowledge of theoretical and applied statistics
  * Proficiency with statistical softwaresuch as R, S-Plus, SAS
  * Effective communication and collaboration skills, including good
    statistical consulting and problem-solving skills
  * Ability to work independently on multiple projects
  * Drug development and/or clinical trial experience will be a plus
  * Basic understanding of molecular biology, cancer, immunology, or genomics
    will be a plus
