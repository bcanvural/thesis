Sr. Data Scientist

Location: Columbia, MD

Type: Full Time

Min. Experience: Manager/Supervisor

Position Summary

The Senior Data Scientist will work both with the Chief Data Scientist and
across business teams to leverage Lotame’s broad foundation of technology and
data assets to deliver data science solutions to Lotame customers. In this
role, you will be responsible for leading a team of data scientists and
engineers in the delivery of products and custom solutions, from concept to
production delivery and maintenance.  

Day to Day Responsibilities

  • Directly lead data science and engineering team members day to day work,
    including daily scrums and oversight of work product results and quality
  • Participate in roadmap development and priority setting for the data
    science team
  • Work with varying Lotame teams and clients to define business needs and
    success criteria, and deliver appropriate scalable solutions.
  • Develop algorithmic solutions to various data science focused challenges
    that utilize very large data sets
  • Generate visualizations to communicate complex data science topics to
    business teams and clients
  • Utilize established open source technologies when available, develop
    innovative and proprietary solutions when necessary

Qualifications

  • Masters in statistics, computer science or other data rich field of study;
    or significant relevant industry experience in machine learning and
    statistics
  • Ability to collaborate with business and engineering teams, both in and
    outside of Lotame, with varying levels of data science and technical know
    how
  • 10+ years experience creating and implementing machine learning algorithms
    for predictive, inference, classification, and analytical use cases
  • 3+ years experience overseeing a team of data scientists and engineers
  • Proven ability to self-start and manage competing priorities by engaging
    with appropriate stakeholders
  • Expertise utilizing big data technologies, such as Hadoop, MapReduce and
    Spark, for both ad hoc analysis and product development
  • Expertise with scripting and/or programming languages, such as Python,
    Scala or Java
  • Experience with cloud services, such as Amazon Web Services
  • Experience with querying relational databases using SQL
