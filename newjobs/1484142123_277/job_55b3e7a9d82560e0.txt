Digital First Media

Data Quality and ETL Analyst

  * Denver, CO
  * Full-time

Company Description

Digital First Media and Adtaxi is looking for a Data Quality and ETL Analyst to
collect, mine, cleanse, and report on analytics data from the source to
production, from our company-wide advertising data warehouse. The Analyst will
design and develop validation models to meet data quality requirements and work
with business stakeholders to understand and document data quality
requirements. They will validate and deliver quality data to analysts and data
scientists as well as work with data engineers to troubleshoot and performance
tune ETL.

What You?ll Be Doing:

  * Check the quality and accuracy of data in the analytics data warehouse and
    identify defects

  * Read and adjust SQL code written by data warehouse engineers for
    troubleshooting, optimization, and performance tuning purposes

  * Follow and validate data from source systems to production

  * Work with business stakeholders to build and implement business
    requirements to identify and correct data discrepancies with SQL and data
    analysis tools

  * Troubleshoot data issues, validate result sets, recommend and implement
    process improvements, provide ad-hoc and scheduled reports and analysis

  * Support SQL and ETL scripts maintenance, changes, and testing

  * Create reports and dashboards for business stakeholders

What We Are Looking For:

  * Skilled at writing and reading complex SQL statements

  * Proficiency in Python or other scripting language

  * Desire to learn and understand data so that data discrepancies are easily
    noticed and identified prior to reporting stage

  * Excellent communication skills in translating requests from the business to
    actionable visual reporting

  * Experience with AdTech systems a plus (e.g., Google Analytics, Google
    AdWords, AppNexus, TradeDesk)

Additional Details

  * This position is located in Denver, CO

  * We are unable to provide sponsorships for H1B Visa applicants

Additional Information

All your information will be kept confidential according to EEO guidelines.
