Digital Data Scientist - Marketing

  * Los Angeles, CA
  * Full Time
  * Analytics and Optimization
  * Mid Level

  *  
  *  
  *  

Join an exciting startup at the right time!

In March 2013, Soothe set out on a mission to make regular massage therapy more
accessible. Three years later, Soothe is now the #1 on-demand massage service
and app, with nearly 7,000 certified massage therapists in almost 43 cities
across the US, UK, Canada, and Australia. We?ve recently raised a $35 million
round of funding and we?re expanding our HQ in Los Angeles. We take great pride
in changing the face of massage therapy for both the clients and therapists. We
deliver hand-picked, background checked, certified & insured massage therapists
to your home, office, hotel, or event space in an hour. We are currently
experiencing remarkable expansion (national and international), and are easily
dominating the health and wellness industry. What better time to join a
startup?

Come join our amazing team and become part of this rapidly growing and evolving
new tech industry!

Description

The Marketing Digital Data Scientist will specialize in analytics across
everything marketing and product related. The ideal candidate is an expert in
SQL, R and Python with experience in using mix-modeling to quantify multi-touch
attribution, ROI, digital channel performance, CAC efficiency, etc. The ideal
candidate is comfortable with using mix-modeling, statistics, and mathematics
to identify optimization opportunities for marketing channels, logistics
algorithms, call center flow, demand and supply matching, event trigger
functions and machine learning systems.

Qualifications

  * Expert command of machine learning, statistical modeling, state-of-the-art
    tools, and engineering best practices.
  * Slice, dice and create KPIs to quantify multiple marketing channels.
  * Optimize the user journey from impression to lead generation to booking.
  * Generate reports to analyze efficiency and operations across all
    departments.
  * Digest large quantities of data to produce insightful and actionable
    strategies on both macro and micro levels.
  * Execute at an expert level for Excel, R, Python, SQL and third party
    analytical tools
  * Data science experience within Tech/Digital and Marketing is a must.
  * A graduate degree in computer science, math, statistics or a related
    quantitative field is a must.

Note: For the right candidate, we are open to relocate the right candidate for
this opportunity. Non-native LA candidates are welcome to apply.
