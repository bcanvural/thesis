Date: Jan 7, 2017

Location: Charlotte, NC, US, 28273

Company: Sealed Air Corporation

Requisition id: 577 

Additional Location(s): None

 

Sealed Air Corporation creates a world that feels, tastes and works better. In
2015, the Company generated revenue of approximately $7.0 billion by helping
our customers achieve their sustainability goals in the face of today’s biggest
social and environmental challenges. Our portfolio of widely recognized brands,
including Cryovac® brand food packaging solutions, Bubble Wrap® brand
cushioning and Diversey® cleaning and hygiene solutions, enables a safer and
less wasteful food supply chain, protects valuable goods shipped around the
world, and improves health through clean environments. Sealed Air has
approximately 23,000 employees who serve customers in 169 countries. To learn
more, visit www.SealedAir.com.

Scope of This Position 

  • Build innovative models for visual analytics, to facilitate a fast and
    efficient discovery of insights from data
  • Execute visual analytical models on outputs from statistical programming
    and machine learning
  • Work with divisional stakeholders and sales teams to build communication
    strategies around how visual analytics will uncover business value

 

Responsibilities 

  • Provide key insights through building succinct dashboard widgets that
    enable customers/internal stakeholders to have an ongoing conversation with
    data
  • Build and maintain visual budgeting/forecasting models for bookings,
    retention, and operational/marketing/sales spend efficiency and provide
    regular reporting, analysis, and insights against these models
  • Work with digital owners to develop an effective communication strategy
    around the role of visual analytics in discovery of insights
  • Explore and examine data multiple sources in order to develop visual
    reporting needs to analyze trends in the KPIs as well as regular reporting
    for assigned customer needs
  • Provide technical analysis to prepare specifications for visual analytics
    and reporting needs – assist with QA and testing of new dashboarding/
    widgets
  • Working under the guidance of project leads, help manage team projects via
    task scheduling and tracking, issue management, status reporting, budget
    management, and stakeholder communication

 

Qualifications 

Required:

  • Must have a Bachelor's Degree
  • Must have 3 - 8 years of related experience
  • Analytics platforms and dashboard development
  • Creative problem skills
  • Integration of dashboards into content management platforms

Preferred: 

  • Master's Degree

 

Requisition id: 577 

Additional Location(s): None

 

All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without
regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, disability or protected
veteran status.

Nearest Major Market: Charlotte
Job Segment: Analytics, Scientific, Database, Data Analyst, Scientist,
Engineering, Management, Technology, Data, Science
